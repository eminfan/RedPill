- **Theoretical Basis**: Extension and expansion of the meta-axiom system
- **Core Logic**: Trial and error and correction are inevitable components of symbiotic Tiandao practice; self-correction and self-iteration are its core vitality
- **Practical Significance**: Providing a theoretical foundation for accommodating mistakes in historical practice, ensuring the theory's dynamic openness

In understanding the historical practice of symbiotic Tiandao, two core concepts that cannot be conflated must be clarified:

## 12.4 Gene-Meme Synergy and Group Combat Effectiveness: The Korean War Case

### Core Question

> Why do certain groups demonstrate combat effectiveness far exceeding expectations under extreme conditions? Under what conditions does this combat effectiveness decay?

Tiandao theory's answer: **The root of group combat effectiveness is not abstract "spirit" or "faith," but the synergy between gene encoding and meme encoding. The higher the synergy, the more efficient the conversion of individual energy toward group goals; when synergy declines, combat effectiveness decays.**

### 12.4.1 Source of Korean War Soldiers' Combat Effectiveness: High Gene-Meme Synergy

#### Gene Layer Support

The gene encoding of Korean War soldiers provided three layers of underlying drive:

1. **Group Defense Instinct**: Gene group evolution theory holds that individuals have strong defensive responses to situations where "foreign groups threaten this group's survival." US forces approaching the Yalu River directly triggered the gene-level defense instinct of "homeland invaded"—this was not educated but encoded in genes.

2. **Kin Protection Drive**: Behind the soldiers were family, land, and villages. Protecting kin is the gene's most底层 hard-coding, requiring no ideological mobilization whatsoever.

3. **Group Belonging Need**: Humans are group animals; the sense of belonging to a group is a gene-encoded survival strategy. The comrade relationships in the military and the neighbor relationships in hometowns constituted gene-level belonging anchors.

#### Meme Layer Support

New China's meme system was highly synergistic with gene encoding:

1. **"Defend Home and Country" Narrative**: This narrative was effective not because it was "noble," but because it **precisely connected with the gene-level group defense instinct**. "Home" corresponds to kin protection; "Country" corresponds to group belonging. The meme did not create new driving forces but directionally focused the existing driving forces of genes.

2. **Material Foundation of Land Reform**: Soldiers had just received land—this was a direct improvement in gene-level "survival resources." The meme layer said "defend the fruits of victory"; the gene layer said "defend my land"—both pointed in exactly the same direction.

3. **Group Cohesion from Egalitarian Relations**: The egalitarian relationships within the PLA (officers and soldiers as equals, military and civilians as equals) caused the meme layer's "comrade" narrative and the gene layer's "kin" perception to叠加—comrades were both "comrades" (meme) and "brothers" (gene).

**Conclusion: The fundamental reason for the Korean War soldiers' strong combat effectiveness is that gene encoding and meme encoding were highly synergistic across all key dimensions, with almost no internal friction in converting individual energy toward group goals.**

### 12.4.2 Combat Effectiveness Decay Conditions: Gene-Meme Synergy Decline

Combat effectiveness is not constant. When the synergy between gene encoding and meme encoding declines, combat effectiveness inevitably decays. There are five specific decay paths:

#### Path One: Meme-Gene Interest Divergence

When the actions demanded by the meme system no longer serve gene-level interests, synergy breaks.

- **Typical Scenario**: War objectives shift from "defend home and country" (gene interest: protect kin and survival resources) to "expedition to foreign lands" (gene interest: unrelated or negatively correlated)
- **Decay Mechanism**: The gene layer provides no driving force; individuals must sustain action entirely through memes, and energy conversion efficiency drops sharply
- **Historical Case**: US Vietnam War—"preventing communist expansion" as a meme objective was unrelated to American soldiers' gene interests (protecting homeland), leading to continuously decaying combat effectiveness

#### Path Two: Meme Promise Unfulfilled

When the benefits promised by the meme system remain unfulfilled over time, meme credibility declines and synergy collapses.

- **Typical Scenario**: Promising to "defend the fruits of victory," but post-war land is re-concentrated and resource distribution is unfair
- **Decay Mechanism**: The gene layer discovers that "defending" did not produce the expected results; next time the meme mobilizes, the gene layer no longer responds
- **Prediction**: If a group fails to fulfill the "home" promise in "defend home and country" after war—such as land being re-consolidated and bottom-layer living conditions deteriorating—then the same meme mobilization will be largely ineffective in the next crisis

#### Path Three: Meme System Internal Contradictions

When the meme system itself develops logical contradictions, individuals experience encoding-level confusion, and synergy declines.

- **Typical Scenario**: The meme system simultaneously promotes "serve the people" and "let some get rich first," with the latter undermining the former
- **Decay Mechanism**: Individuals cannot determine the meme's true direction, producing hesitation and internal friction when acting
- **Prediction**: Meme system self-consistency is a prerequisite for maintaining synergy. Once irreconcilable contradictions appear within the meme, group cohesion will irreversibly decline

#### Path Four: Gene Layer Survival Pressure Overload

When gene-level survival needs are excessively suppressed, gene encoding breaks through meme constraints.

- **Typical Scenario**: Prolonged warfare causing extreme individual fatigue, hunger, and heavy casualties; gene-level survival instinct overwhelms meme-level mission calling
- **Decay Mechanism**: Gene encoding has higher priority than meme encoding (survival is the first axiom); when survival itself is threatened, meme constraints fail
- **Historical Pattern**: Any military's combat effectiveness has a material bottom line—when food and ammunition run out, no amount of faith can sustain combat effectiveness

#### Path Five: Competing Alternative Memes

When new memes enter the system and compete with existing memes for connection to gene encoding, synergy is diluted.

- **Typical Scenario**: Consumerism, individualism, and other memes enter, competing with "collectivism" and "dedication" memes
- **Decay Mechanism**: Gene-level driving forces are contested by multiple memes; individuals oscillate among multiple encodings, and action consistency declines
- **Prediction**: In information-open societies, meme competition is the norm. The key to maintaining combat effectiveness is not eliminating alternative memes but ensuring the core meme's synergy with gene interests always exceeds that of alternative memes

Based on the above analysis, the following theorems can be derived:

#### Theorem One: Gene-Meme Synergy Theorem
