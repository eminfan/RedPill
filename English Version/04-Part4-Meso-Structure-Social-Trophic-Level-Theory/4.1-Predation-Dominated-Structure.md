## 9.2 Operating Mechanism: A Precision Extraction Pump

The predatory structure is essentially a **"negative entropy extraction pump."** Through three subsystems, it continuously extracts survival resources from the bottom layer to the top layer.

### 1. Violence Pump (Foundation Layer)
- **Principle**: Directly use coercive force (military, police, prisons) to ensure unidirectional resource flow.
- **Function**: Ensure the bottom layer dare not cut off the energy flow through violent resistance.
- **Energy Cost**: High. If relying solely on violence, governance costs are extremely high (Qin Dynasty model), and the system is highly unstable.

### 2. Institutional Pump (Middle Layer)
- **Principle**: Through laws, taxation, and financial rules, make predation **legalized** and **concealed**.
- **Common Forms**:
    - **Land consolidation/rent**: Possessing the means of production, appropriating surplus value without compensation.
    - **Financial seigniorage**: Diluting bottom-layer wealth through inflation.
    - **Franchise rights**: Obtaining excess profits through administrative monopoly (salt and iron monopoly).
- **Function**: Reduce the friction coefficient of predation, making the exploited believe "that's just how the rules are."

### 3. Meme Pump (Top Layer)
- **Principle**: Through culture, religion, and education, implant **"obedience memes."**
- **Common Rhetoric**:
    - "Divine right of kings" / "Mandate of Heaven"
    - "Endure the hardest hardships to become the highest achievers"
    - "Your poverty is because you didn't work hard enough"
- **Function**: Make the battery love the battery compartment. This is the highest form of predation—lowest energy cost, most lasting effect.

- **Function**:

    - **Manufacture Hope**: Make the remaining 99.99% believe "I didn't succeed because I'm not capable," rather than "the system is broken."

## 9.4 Evolutionary Destiny: Thermodynamic Collapse

Despite designing numerous stabilization mechanisms, predatory structures cannot escape the **"historical cycle law."** This is not because emperors are foolish, but because of **physical laws**.

### Collapse Process

1.  **Early Stage (Establishment)**: The new regime resets resource distribution through violence (killing the rich to aid the poor), parasitic coefficient $Pc$ is low, and the system rapidly accumulates negative entropy through "recuperation."
2.  **Middle Stage (Enrichment)**: The enrichment layer uses rule advantages to continuously increase extraction intensity. The transmission layer (bureaucracy) begins to expand and retain energy. $Pc$ gradually rises.
3.  **Late Stage (Rigidity)**:
    - The top layer has annexed the vast majority of land/capital.
    - The middle layer has retained the vast majority of tax revenue.
    - The production layer's (peasants/workers) energy input < minimum consumption needed for survival.
4.  **Terminal Stage (Heat Death/Explosion)**:
    - **Heat Death**: If the bottom layer is compliant, the system enters a rigid state, population drops sharply, and civilization stagnates (e.g., Wei-Jin Southern and Northern Dynasties).
    - **Explosion**: If the bottom layer resists, the system undergoes violent dissipation (peasant uprisings), and energy is reshuffled.

### Conclusion
**Predation-dominated structures have no future.**
They either cycle through periodic collapses or rot in prolonged rigidity.
Unless—**anti-entropy work** is introduced, leaping to a symbiotic Tiandao.
