# 4 Symbiosis-Dominated Structure

## 10.1 Core Logic: A Proactively Designed Sustainable Form

### Core Proposition

**Symbiosis-dominated structure is a sustainable development structure that humans proactively design and construct after cognizing the objective laws of social operation, with the long-term survival and evolutionary development maximization of the group as the core goal.**

### Design Principles

1. **Systemic Principle**: Design structure from the perspective of the overall system, not local optimization
2. **Fairness Principle**: Ensure opportunity fairness in resource distribution, eliminate institutionalized predation
3. **Efficiency Principle**: Improve resource utilization efficiency through reasonable division of labor and collaboration
4. **Sustainability Principle**: Ensure the system's long-term stability and evolutionary capability
5. **Adaptability Principle**: Ability to self-adjust and optimize with environmental changes

### Operating Logic

- **Bidirectional Resource Circulation**: Surplus resources form a positive cycle within the system, feeding back to the production layer
- **Open and Transparent Rules**: Resource distribution rules are open and transparent, subject to oversight
- **Power Checks and Balances**: Achieve effective power checks and balances through institutional design
- **Innovation Incentives**: Establish mechanisms that encourage innovation, releasing individual vitality

## 10.2 Key Characteristics: Anti-Predation and Positive Resource Circulation

### 1. Zeroing Out Institutionalized Predation

- **Core Characteristic**: An ownership structure with public ownership of the means of production as the mainstay and diverse forms of ownership developing together as the core, fundamentally abolishing uncompensated predation based on means of production and power monopoly
- **Implementation Methods**:
  - Property rights system reform: Clarify public ownership or fair distribution of means of production
  - Distribution system design: Implement distribution according to work as the mainstay, with multiple distribution methods coexisting
  - Oversight mechanisms: Build a diversified rigid oversight system to regulate power operation
- **Result**: Individual/group returns are solely linked to labor contribution, management value, technological innovation, and risk-bearing, completely closing institutional channels for unearned income

### 2. Bidirectional Positive Circulation of Resource Flow

- **Core Characteristic**: Surplus resources created by the production layer no longer unidirectionally enrich the top layer but flow back to the public domain through the public distribution system
- **Flow Path**:
  - Production layer creates surplus resources
  - Part of surplus resources used for public services (education, healthcare, infrastructure, innovation)
  - Public services feed back to the production layer, improving production efficiency
  - Forms a "production-distribution-feedback-reproduction" positive closed loop
- **Result**: All members share development outcomes, and the system achieves sustainable development

### 3. Opportunity Fairness in Class Mobility

- **Core Characteristic**: The sole standard for individual level ascension is labor contribution, capability level, and innovation outcomes, completely unrelated to background, intergenerational resources, or bloodline
- **Implementation Methods**:
  - Education equity: Ensure fair distribution of educational resources
  - Employment fairness: Eliminate employment discrimination, establish fair selection mechanisms
  - Social security: Establish social assistance and safety net mechanisms
  - Entrepreneurship support: Provide entrepreneurial opportunities and resources for bottom-layer groups
- **Result**: Smooth class mobility, full release of social vitality

### 4. Service-Oriented Reconstruction of Level Functions

- **Core Characteristic**: The core functions of the enrichment and transmission layers fundamentally transform from "rule-making and resource predation" to "serving group development and bearing system risks"
- **Implementation Methods**:
  - Role repositioning: Clarify the service functions of each level
  - Incentive mechanism adjustment: Establish incentive systems linked to service performance
  - Oversight system improvement: Ensure power operates in the sunlight
- **Result**: The transmission layer returns to its core function as a "resource regulating valve," and the enrichment layer becomes the system's "risk bearer" and "innovation leader"

### 5. Dynamic Balance of Efficiency and Fairness

- **Core Characteristic**: No egalitarianism; clearly protect reasonable income differences based on labor, management, innovation, and risk-bearing, while curbing polarization through tax regulation, social security, and other mechanisms
- **Implementation Methods**:
  - Primary distribution: Focus on efficiency, encourage innovation and contribution
  - Secondary distribution: Focus on fairness, regulate income gaps through taxation
  - Tertiary distribution: Encourage philanthropy, promote social harmony
- **Result**: Achieve dynamic unity of "fairness as the floor, efficiency as the ceiling"

## 10.3 Implementation Conditions: Institutional Design and Mechanism Guarantees

### 1. Property Rights System

- **Core Requirement**: Clarify public ownership or fair distribution of means of production, eliminate monopolistic possession by individuals or small groups
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Public ownership as the mainstay: Important resources and key sectors implement public ownership
  - Diverse ownership coexisting: Encourage healthy development of non-public economy
  - Property rights protection: Legally protect all types of legitimate property rights

### 2. Oversight Mechanisms

- **Core Requirement**: Build a "universal oversight + internal self-renewal + third-party evaluation" diversified rigid oversight system
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Democratic oversight: Expand citizen participation, enhance oversight breadth
  - Institutional oversight: Establish and improve power constraint and oversight mechanisms
  - Public opinion oversight: Leverage the oversight role of media and social public opinion
  - Self-oversight: Establish internal self-correction mechanisms within the system

### 3. Distribution System

- **Core Requirement**: Implement "distribution according to work as the mainstay, with multiple distribution methods coexisting," balancing efficiency and fairness
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Labor compensation: Ensure labor compensation matches labor contribution
  - Factor participation in distribution: Allow capital, technology, management, and other factors to participate in distribution
  - Social security: Establish a social security system covering all people
  - Tax regulation: Regulate excessively high income through progressive taxes

### 4. Mobility Mechanisms

- **Core Requirement**: Advance education equity, employment fairness, and establish sound social assistance and upward mobility channels
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Education reform: Promote balanced distribution of educational resources, ensure education fairness
  - Employment services: Establish a fair employment market, eliminate employment discrimination
  - Social assistance: Establish a multi-tier social assistance system
  - Entrepreneurship support: Provide funding, technology, and policy support for entrepreneurs

### 5. Innovation Mechanisms

- **Core Requirement**: Increase investment in basic research and technological innovation, improve innovation outcome transformation mechanisms
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Technology investment: Increase R&D expenditure, support basic research
  - Talent cultivation: Establish an innovative talent cultivation system
  - Outcome transformation: Improve scientific and technological achievement transformation mechanisms, promote industry-academia-research integration
  - Innovation incentives: Establish and improve innovation incentive mechanisms, protect intellectual property

### 6. Cultural Mechanisms

- **Core Requirement**: Cultivate cultural values centered on symbiosis, cooperation, and innovation
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Values guidance: Advocate collectivism, cooperative spirit, and innovation consciousness
  - Education cultivation: Integrate symbiotic concepts into the education system
  - Cultural inheritance: Inherit and innovate excellent traditional culture
  - Public opinion guidance: Create a social atmosphere conducive to symbiotic development
