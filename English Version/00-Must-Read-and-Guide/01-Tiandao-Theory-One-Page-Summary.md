## II. Meso Structure: What Is Society?

**Society is an "energy flow trophic pyramid."**

```
        ▲ Enrichment Layer (makes rules, legally concentrates resources)
       ╱ ╲
      ╱   ╲ Transmission Layer (executes rules, retains partial resources)
     ╱─────╲
    ╱       ╲
   ╱─────────╲ Production Layer (creates value, surplus is extracted)
```

**Core Theorem**: Resources naturally flow from the Production Layer to the Enrichment Layer, just as energy flows unidirectionally through a food chain.

**Key Indicator — Parasitic Coefficient**:
```
Pc = (Enrichment Layer + Transmission Layer consumption) / Surplus created by Production Layer
```
- Pc < 0.5: System is healthy and sustainable
- Pc > 0.8: System is unstable, high risk of collapse
- Pc > 1.0: Production Layer cannot cover costs, collapse is inevitable
