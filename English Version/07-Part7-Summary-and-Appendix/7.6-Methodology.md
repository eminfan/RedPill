# Methodology

This project employs a unique **"physics-biology-society" interdisciplinary reductionist** methodology, whose core lies in reducing human social phenomena to underlying physical and biological laws and deriving conclusions through strict logical deduction.

This methodology is called the **"Tiandao Analysis Method,"** and its operating mechanism is as follows:

### I. Core Research Methods

The research method of this project can be summarized as a dual path of **"axiomatic deduction + clairvoyance deconstruction"**:

#### 1. Axiomatic Deduction
This is the cornerstone of theory construction. The project does not rely on traditional sociological observation or questionnaire surveys but starts from **unshakeable natural science axioms** and proceeds with logical derivation.

*   **Meta-Starting Point**: Based on the **Second Law of Thermodynamics (entropy increase)** and **evolutionary biology**.
    *   **Axiom 1: Entropy increase is irreversible** — The universe overall tends toward disorder; survival requires continuously acquiring energy (negative entropy).
    *   **Axiom 2: Resource scarcity** — Negative entropy flow (resources) is limited in specific space-time, leading to zero-sum games.
    *   **Axiom 3: Evolutionary selection** — Only structures that can effectively acquire resources and reproduce can survive.
*   **Derivation Logic**:
    *   Because survival requires negative entropy → individuals must compete for resources.
    *   Because individual power is limited → groups must be formed (collaborative value-addition).
    *   Because collaboration requires rules → "memes" (culture/institutions) emerge to constrain instincts.
    *   Because human nature seeks profit and avoids harm → those who control rules inevitably tend toward predation (social stratification).
    *   **Conclusion**: Social classes, moral laws, and state apparatuses are not human designs but **inevitable mathematical solutions** of system evolution.

#### 2. Clairvoyance Framework (Ling-Shi Framework)
This is the **"cognitive deconstruction tool"** used for analyzing specific social phenomena, mandatorily enforced in the project. It requires that when analyzing any phenomenon, surface moral narratives must be stripped away to reach the underlying interest structure.

*   **Structural Deconstruction**: Decomposing social phenomena into **production layer, transmission layer, enrichment layer** (social trophic level theory).
*   **Energy Tracking (Follow the Energy)**: Not looking at what people "say" (surface narrative), only watching where resources/energy "flow."
*   **Semantic De-anchoring**: Questioning and stripping the hypnotic effects of phrases like "for your own good" and "for the greater good," restoring their essence as **"meme domestication tools."**
*   **Incentive Reverse Engineering**: Inferring the true intent of institutional design by working backward from "who ultimately benefits."

### II. How Knowledge Is Acquired and Conclusions Reached

This project acquires knowledge and verifies conclusions through the following **closed-loop process**:

1.  **Establishing the Axiom System (Origin)**
    *   Establishing physics (thermodynamics) and biology (evolutionary theory) as unquestionable meta-starting points.
    *   Rejecting any a priori assumptions like "natural rights" or "inherent human goodness."

2.  **Model Construction (Modeling)**
    *   Constructing the **"gene-meme dual encoding"** model: Humans are hosts for genes (biological instincts) and memes (cultural software).
    *   Constructing the **"social trophic level"** model: Society is a pyramid structure of energy flow.

3.  **Logical Deduction**
    *   Under the premise of limited resources, deducing the inevitable patterns of group behavior.
    *   For example: Deriving that the **"historical cycle law"** is not an accident but an inevitable physical process of dissipative structure collapse caused by system entropy accumulation.

4.  **Empirical Verification**
    *   Using **historical cases** (e.g., Qing Dynasty rule, Japanese militarism) and **contemporary phenomena** (e.g., American progressive education, Soviet planned economy) for verification.
    *   Verification standard: Does the "energy flow direction" derived from theory match historical facts?
    *   **Case**: When analyzing "progressive education," not looking at its "respecting nature" propaganda, but at its results—public education through "burden reduction" diminished the bottom layer's ability to access quality educational resources, thereby solidifying class (energy flowing to the enrichment layer), verifying the "Class Solidification Theorem."

5.  **Conclusion Generation**
    *   Conclusions are often **counter-intuitive** and **de-moralized**.
    *   For example: Morality is not sacred truth but an **"algorithm"** for reducing intra-group collaboration costs; the state is not a community but a tool for class rule (under predatory Tiandao).

### Summary
The research method of this project is **"examining human society with the austere gaze of physics."** It does not ask "how the world should be" (normative), only asks "how the world inevitably is under physical and biological laws" (positive). Through **axiomatic deduction** ensuring logical rigor, and through **clairvoyance deconstruction** ensuring penetration of reality.
