> **Note**: This glossary not only provides academic definitions but also includes **"💡 Plain Understanding"** to help readers cross cognitive thresholds and quickly grasp the theoretical core.

## I. Foundational Concepts (Physics and Philosophy)

### Entropy Increase
*   **Academic Definition**: The universal tendency described by the Second Law of Thermodynamics, whereby in a closed system, disorder (entropy) always tends to increase, ultimately leading to heat death.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"The universe's demolition crew."** If not maintained, rooms get messy, hot water cools, people age, and dynasties collapse. Chaos is the universe's default; order is a special state requiring energy to maintain.

### Dissipative Structure
*   **Academic Definition**: An open system far from equilibrium that maintains its ordered structure by continuously ingesting negative entropy (matter, energy, information) from outside and discharging positive entropy.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"A lifeform sailing against the current."** Life, cities, and nations are all dissipative structures. Like a vortex, they must constantly ingest energy and excrete waste to maintain their shape. Once input stops (food/power cut off), they disintegrate (die).

### Negative Entropy
*   **Academic Definition**: A concept proposed by Schrödinger, referring to energy or information that can reduce a system's entropy value (increase order).
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Life potion."** For humans, it's food; for civilization, it's oil, technology, and new ideas.

## II. Micro Mechanisms (Human Nature, Genes, and Memes)

### Gene-Meme Dual Encoding
*   **Academic Definition**: Humans are composites jointly constructed by **genes (biological hereditary information)** and **memes (cultural hereditary information)**. The two co-evolve within individuals while also engaging in game-theoretic conflict.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Hardware and software."** Genes are your factory hardware, only caring about survival and reproduction; memes are the software you install afterward (like morality, patriotism), managing social collaboration. The "inner conflict" in your head is often the hardware wanting meat while the software wants dieting.

### Meme
*   **Academic Definition**: The basic unit of cultural transmission (e.g., ideas, melodies, catchphrases), which replicates and spreads between human brains through imitation, possessing evolutionary characteristics of variation and selection.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Brain virus."** An idea (like "diamonds represent eternity") infects everyone like a virus, controlling your behavior (making you pay money for a rock).

### Meme Domestication
*   **Academic Definition**: The process by which social systems, through specific meme implantation, suppress individual biological instincts and transform discrete individual energy into group order.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Taming wolves into dogs."** Society uses concepts like "glory," "success," and "dedication" to make you suppress your animal nature (selfishness, laziness) and obediently work 996, generating power for the collective.

### Selective Redundancy
*   **Academic Definition**: Unnecessarily complex rules set by the system beyond what is needed to solve problems, functioning to screen for high-compliance individuals.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Compliance test."** Why must forms be so tedious? Why are there so many drinking table rules? Not for efficiency, but to see if you obey. Those who can't stand it are filtered out; those who remain are useful parts.

### Semantic Anchoring
*   **Academic Definition**: By monopolizing the definition rights of specific vocabulary, locking individuals' cognitive frameworks so they cannot perceive the system's true logic.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Labeling."** Calling "exploitation" a "blessing," calling "consumption" "being good to yourself." Once words are defined, your mind is locked.

## III. Meso Structure (Social Trophic Levels)

### Social Trophic Level
*   **Academic Definition**: Social stratification structure formed based on energy (resource) flow direction. Position depends on the individual's location in the resource flow chain (production, transmission, or enrichment).
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Food chain position."** Are you a grass-eater (production layer), a meat-eater (enrichment layer), or someone helping transport grass to the meat-eaters (transmission layer)?

### Production Layer (First Trophic Level)
*   **Academic Definition**: Groups that directly create negative entropy (value) but have a net output of energy.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Batteries."** Farmers, workers, ordinary white-collar workers. Working the most, getting the least, the cornerstone maintaining system operation.

### Transmission Layer (Second Trophic Level)
*   **Academic Definition**: Intermediate groups responsible for executing rules, maintaining order, controlling energy flow rates, and intercepting some resources.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Pipes and valves."** Managers, middle-level cadres, clerks. Not producing directly, but managing producers and collecting tolls along the way.

### Enrichment Layer (Third Trophic Level)
*   **Academic Definition**: Top-layer groups that make rules and legally enrich the largest proportion of surplus resources.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Harvesters."** Big capitalists, top power holders. They design the game rules so resources automatically flow into their pockets.

### Parasitic Coefficient (Pc)
*   **Academic Definition**: The ratio of total energy consumed by the enrichment layer and transmission layer to the surplus energy created by the production layer.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Bloodsucking index."** The higher this number, the more idle people are being supported, and the harder workers toil. Above 1.0, the system should be collapsing.

## IV. Macro Evolution (Tiandao and History)

### Predatory Tiandao
*   **Academic Definition**: Spontaneous order without top-level anti-entropy design, following the physical instinct of "predatory profit-seeking," inevitably leading to the historical cycle law of "class solidification—system collapse."
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"The death spiral of jungle law."** Everyone hacks at each other by instinct, the strong take all, and finally flip the table to start over.

### Symbiotic Tiandao
*   **Academic Definition**: Anti-entropy construction based on rational cognition, curbing predation through institutional design, achieving symbiotic resource circulation and sustained civilizational upgrading.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"A designed civilized garden."** Not only making the cake bigger but also setting rules for dividing it, so the strong can't casually bully the weak, and everyone plays together longer.

### Placebo Channel
*   **Academic Definition**: Extremely low-probability upward channels retained in predatory Tiandao, functioning to relieve bottom-layer anxiety and maintain system stability, rather than changing structure.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Lottery tickets."** Imperial examinations, talent shows, get-rich-quick myths. Making 99% of people think "I didn't succeed because I didn't work hard enough," so they don't blame the system.

### Tittytainment
*   **Academic Definition**: A concept proposed by Brzezinski, referring to the dissipation of marginal groups' excess energy and dissatisfaction through low-cost, high-sensory-stimulation entertainment content.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Mental pacifier."** Endless short videos, soap operas, trending gossip. Making you feel good, making you laugh, making you forget to think about why you're poor.

## V. Methodology and Cognitive Tools

### Clairvoyance (Ling-Shi)
*   **Academic Definition**: A dimension-reduction observation technique that strips away surface narratives and moral filters to directly perceive the system's structure, incentives, and energy flow.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"X-ray cheat."** Don't listen to what they say (for the dream), only watch what they do (took the money). Seeing through at a glance who's drawing the pie and who's eating it.

### Empty Boat (Xu-Zhou)
*   **Academic Definition**: Originating from Zhuangzi, referring to a survival strategy of maintaining internal cognitive sovereignty (inner non-transformation) while maintaining external behavioral compatibility (outer transformation) after seeing through the system's truth.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Being awake while pretending to sleep."** I know this world is a ramshackle troupe, but I'll play along with your act, not being stubborn, not consuming myself, quietly accumulating my own strength.

### Red Pill
*   **Academic Definition**: Originating from *The Matrix*, metaphor for the cruel but real truth.
*   **💡 Plain Understanding**: **"Awakening pill."** Swallow it, and you'll see the truth of the world (painful); don't swallow it, and you can continue living happily in the dream (false).
