## Core Proposition Extraction

### Proposition One: Memes Are Genes at the Cultural Level

Memes are any information units that can replicate, propagate, and mutate in human brains. Meme survival does not depend on whether it conforms to facts, only on its transmission capability. Memes form complexes; multiple complementary memes combine to form extremely strong control closed loops.

### Proposition Two: Domestication Is Three-Layer Locking

Domestication achieves control through a three-layer structure:
- **Emotional Anchoring**: Makes you "dare not disbelieve"
- **Identity Binding**: Makes you "cannot disbelieve"
- **Meaning Conferral**: Makes you "don't want to disbelieve"

Once three-layer locking is complete, the individual completely becomes a meme's operating terminal.

### Proposition Three: Individuals Are Dual Hosts of Dual Groups

Individuals are dual hosts of gene groups and meme groups. Gene groups write underlying instinct encoding through heredity; meme groups pre-install cognitive operating systems through socialization. Meme groups may require hosts to sacrifice gene interests to serve meme propagation.

### Proposition Four: Meme Groups Form Super-Individuals

Meme groups form super-individuals at the group scale, possessing integration capabilities that transcend any single individual. Super-individuals achieve group integration through three major mechanisms: group crisis activation, consensus framework solidification, and self-purification with boundary maintenance.

### Proposition Five: Clairvoyance Can Penetrate Domestication

Clairvoyance is the cognitive ability to penetrate meme domestication—seeing through emotional manipulation, seeing through narrative disguise, seeing through identity binding. Clairvoyance achieves cognitive penetration through three laws (redundancy shielding, semantic anchoring, entropy-energy conversion) and triple penetration (emotional, narrative, identity).

### Proposition Six: Anti-Domestication Is Systematic Practice

Anti-domestication is systematic practice, including five steps: cognitive audit, emotional unbinding, framework reconstruction, energy recovery, and active choice. The ultimate goal of anti-domestication is achieving cognitive sovereignty—knowing what influences you and being able to autonomously choose to accept or reject.

### Proposition Seven: Awakening Is Sevenfold Transformation

Awakening is a sevenfold transformation from outside to inside, from shallow to deep: energy level, relationship level, survival level, cognitive level, emotional level, behavioral level, and ultimate level. Each level of awakening corresponds to liberation from the control of specific meme groups.

## Boundaries and Limitations of the Theory

### Boundary One: Not Negating Everything

Meme Group Evolution and Domestication Theory does not negate all memes, but examines all memes and autonomously chooses what to accept. Some memes (such as basic collaboration rules, reasonable moral norms) are beneficial to both individuals and groups.

### Boundary Two: Not Escaping Society

Awakening is not leaving society, but participating in society in an awake manner. Awakened people know what they're participating in, why they're participating, and can choose to withdraw at any time.

### Boundary Three: Not a One-Time Event

Awakening is not a one-time event, but a continuous process. Meme group control is omnipresent and continuous; anti-domestication also needs to be ongoing.

### Boundary Four: Not a Source of Superiority

Awakening is not an identity superiority, but a cognitive state. Thinking you're "more awake" than others is itself a new cognitive trap.
