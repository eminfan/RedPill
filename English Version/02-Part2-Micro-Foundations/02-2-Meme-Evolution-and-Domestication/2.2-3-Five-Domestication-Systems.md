# 4 Five Domestication Systems

Meme domestication is not a single mechanism, but a systematic engineering project composed of five domestication systems. These five systems cooperate with each other to jointly achieve comprehensive control over individuals at different levels of society.

## Five Domestication Systems Overview

| Domestication System | Core Function | Target Group | Typical Memes |
|---------------------|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| Moral Domestication | Reduce governance costs | Bottom-layer populace | Hard work leads to wealth, suffering is a blessing |
| Romantic Love Domestication | Stabilize family structure | Both sexes | True love is unique, marriage is sacred |
| Consumption Domestication | Harvest wealth and energy | All groups | Consumption upgrade, identity symbols |
| Success Ideology Domestication | Maintain system stability | Those desiring upward mobility | Hard work leads to reversal |
| Collectivist Domestication | Extract individual energy | All citizens | Patriotism, honor, mission |

## I. Moral Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Reduce governance costs, stabilize bottom-layer order. Make the bottom layer willingly accept being harvested—no resistance, no questioning, no seeking of class mobility.

### Core Mechanism

Moral domestication packages "obedience" as "virtue" and defines "resistance" as "evil," making people actively accept the fate of being harvested.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. Hard Work Leads to Wealth

**Surface Meaning**: As long as you work hard, you can gain wealth

**Actual Function**:
- Makes people believe in the rationality of the existing distribution system
- Attributes poverty to individual lack of effort, avoiding structural injustice
- Consumes bottom-layer energy, reducing possibility of resistance

**Essence**: A placebo meme defending the existing order

#### 2. Suffering Is a Blessing

**Surface Meaning**: Those willing to suffer will receive blessings

**Actual Function**:
- Makes the bottom layer actively give up rights
- Suppresses individual rights-awareness
- Reduces conflict incidence

**Essence**: A self-persuasion mechanism rationalizing exploitation

#### 3. Loyalty and Filial Piety

**Surface Meaning**: Maintain loyalty to authority and filial piety to elders

**Actual Function**:
- Strengthens legitimacy of hierarchical systems
- Facilitates elder-to-younger resource extraction
- Maintains intergenerational oppression structures

**Essence**: Moral packaging for upward energy flow within families

#### 4. Patience and Non-Contention

**Surface Meaning**: Be patient, don't fight

**Actual Function**:
- Makes the bottom layer give up fighting for rights
- Maintains existing resource distribution patterns
- Reduces system friction costs

**Essence**: A rhetorical tool for reducing governance costs

## II. Romantic Love Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Bind the sexes, stabilize families, ensure offspring rearing. Serve gene continuation and social structural stability.

### Core Mechanism

Romantic love domestication sacralizes "love" and stigmatizes "being single," making people actively enter marriage and family structures, undertaking the massive investment of raising offspring.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. One Person for a Lifetime

**Surface Meaning**: True love is unique and eternal

**Actual Function**:
- Suppresses individual exploration of diverse relationships
- Controls number of sexual partners to a minimum
- Ensures certain parental investment in offspring

**Essence**: A tool for reducing infidelity rates and stabilizing spousal relationships

#### 2. Sacrifice for Love

**Surface Meaning**: True love requires giving and sacrifice

**Actual Function**:
- Rationalizes one party's resource extraction from another
- Equates "sacrifice" with "proof of love"
- Suppresses individual self-interest protection awareness

**Essence**: Moral packaging for unequal energy flow in intimate relationships

#### 3. Marriage Is a Sacred Duty

**Surface Meaning**: Marriage is a sacred life mission

**Actual Function**:
- Urges individuals to enter marriage structures
- Stigmatizes single status
- Ensures stability of population reproduction

**Essence**: Infrastructure for social structural stability

## III. Consumption Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Stimulate desire, create involution, harvest wealth. Make people work for commodities, perpetually in debt, with their own energy continuously extracted.

### Core Mechanism

Consumption domestication creates false needs, binds human value with commodity consumption, making people constantly pursue more consumption, falling into a desire cycle that can never be satisfied.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. You Deserve Better

**Surface Meaning**: You deserve better commodities

**Actual Function**:
- Stimulates pursuit of higher-grade commodities
- Rationalizes超前 consumption
- Continuously drives consumption demand

**Essence**: A marketing tool stimulating consumption desire

#### 2. Loving Yourself Means Buying

**Surface Meaning**: Consumption is a way of self-care

**Actual Function**:
- Binds consumption with self-worth
- Uses shopping to relieve negative emotions
- Forms a compulsive emotion-consumption cycle

**Essence**: A domestication mechanism turning people into consumers

#### 3. Taste = Class

**Surface Meaning**: What you consume represents who you are

**Actual Function**:
- Uses commodities to define personal identity
- Stimulates comparative consumption
- Continuously pursues higher "class symbols"

**Essence**: A class game maintaining never-satisfied consumption

#### 4. Not Buying Is Failure

**Surface Meaning**: Not consuming is shameful

**Actual Function**:
- Creates shopping anxiety
- Stigmatizes rational consumption
- Compulsive shopping behavior

**Essence**: A shame tool depriving individuals of rational judgment ability

## IV. Success Ideology Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Create hope for the bottom layer, prevent system collapse. The social trophic level structure determines that the vast majority cannot move upward; success ideology is merely a placebo meme pacifying the bottom layer.

### Core Mechanism

Success ideology domestication creates the illusion that "everyone can succeed," making the bottom layer believe they can achieve class mobility through personal effort, thereby accepting the status quo without questioning the system itself.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. Hard Work Leads to Reversal

**Surface Meaning**: Anyone can succeed if they work hard enough

**Actual Function**:
- Attributes failure to individual lack of effort
- Avoids structural causes of class solidification
- Makes the bottom layer continuously involute rather than resist

**Essence**: A placebo maintaining system stability

#### 2. You're Not Successful Because You're Not Disciplined

**Surface Meaning**: Failure is due to personal quality issues

**Actual Function**:
- Attributes structural difficulties to personal qualities
- Triggers self-condemnation rather than system questioning
- Continuously consumes individual energy in involution

**Essence**: A psychological mechanism shifting system responsibility

#### 3. Humble Origins Produce Great Ones

**Surface Meaning**: Poor families can also produce talents

**Actual Function**:
- Uses extremely rare reversal cases to obscure general patterns
- Makes people believe class mobility is generally possible
- Ignores statistically extremely low probabilities

**Essence**: Survivor bias defending unequal structures

## V. Collectivist Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Drive individuals to sacrifice for the group, maintain super-group competitive advantage. Maximally extract individual energy for the group; the most efficient energy harvesting mechanism.

### Core Mechanism

Collectivist domestication sacralizes "collective interests," making individuals willingly contribute their own energy to the collective, achieving high concentration and unified allocation of energy.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. Patriotism

**Surface Meaning**: Love your country

**Actual Function**:
- Places national interests above personal interests
- Rationalizes individual sacrifice for the nation
- Stimulates hostility and competition toward out-groups

**Essence**: An emotional tool for super-group cohesion

#### 2. Collective Honor

**Surface Meaning**: Collective honor surpasses personal interests

**Actual Function**:
- Makes individuals sacrifice for collective honor
- Uses sense of honor to drive behavior
- Suppresses individual interest demands

**Essence**: Honor packaging for upward energy flow

#### 3. Mission Calling

**Surface Meaning**: Everyone has a special mission

**Actual Function**:
- Confers sacred purpose upon individual lives
- Makes people actively pursue "mission"
- Uses sense of mission to drive behavior

**Essence**: A sacralized narrative for individual energy extraction

#### 4. Sacrifice the Small Self to Complete the Greater Self

**Surface Meaning**: For greater goals, sacrifice the individual

**Actual Function**:
- Sacralizes individual sacrifice
- Rationalizes resource extraction from individuals
- Suppresses individual self-protection awareness

**Essence**: The most efficient energy harvesting rhetoric

## Synergy of the Five Systems

### Systematic Control

```
Moral Domestication (Bottom-layer order)
    ↓
Romantic Love Domestication (Family structure)
    ↓
Consumption Domestication (Wealth harvesting)
    ↓
Success Ideology Domestication (Hope maintenance)
    ↓
Collectivist Domestication (Energy extraction)
    ↓
Comprehensive systematic control
```

### Hierarchical Coverage

- **Bottom Layer**: Moral domestication + Success ideology domestication
- **Middle Layer**: Consumption domestication + Romantic love domestication
- **Top Layer**: Collectivist domestication + Moral domestication

### Complementary Enhancement

- Moral domestication reduces resistance will
- Romantic love domestication stabilizes family structures
- Consumption domestication harvests wealth and energy
- Success ideology domestication maintains system hope
- Collectivist domestication extracts individual energy

## Practical Application

### Identifying the Five Domestication Systems

1. **Moral Domestication**: Are "should/shouldn't" being used to suppress rational judgment?
2. **Romantic Love Domestication**: Is being single stigmatized? Is "true love" used to rationalize unequal relationships?
3. **Consumption Domestication**: Is consumption used to define self-worth? Are you trapped in a never-satisfied shopping cycle?
4. **Success Ideology Domestication**: Is failure attributed to individuals? Are structural injustices avoided?
5. **Collectivist Domestication**: Are collective interests placed above personal interests? Have you sacrificed personal development for the collective?

### Principles for Resisting Domestication

1. **Moral Independence**: Don't be kidnapped by "should/shouldn't"; use rationality to judge behavioral consequences
2. **Emotional Autonomy**: Don't be defined by "true love"; independently decide the form of intimate relationships
3. **Consumption Rationality**: Don't let commodities define self-worth; live within your means
4. **Structural Cognition**: See the structural causes of class solidification; don't attribute failure to individuals
5. **Individual Value**: Recognize that individual value doesn't depend on the collective; rationally evaluate collective interests
