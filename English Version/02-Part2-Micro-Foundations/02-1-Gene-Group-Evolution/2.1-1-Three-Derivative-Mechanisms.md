# 3 Human Nature: Gene-Meme Dual Encoding

## 3.4 Derivative Mechanisms of Dual Encoding: Instinct, Domestication, and Gaming

### Core Proposition

**The interaction between genes (instincts) and memes (culture) is not always harmonious symbiosis, but rather continuous dynamic gaming within the individual. This gaming has derived three core mechanisms of human society: desire domestication, cultural variation, and system immunity.**

---

### I. Desire Domestication Mechanism (Immediate Feedback vs. Delayed Gratification)

#### 1.1 Source of Conflict

- **Gene Logic (Instinct)**: Pursues **immediate feedback**.
    - Mechanism: Dopamine reward circuit.
    - Goal: Ensure immediate survival and reproduction (e.g., eat sugar when you see it, pursue the opposite sex when you see them).
- **Meme Logic (Culture)**: Pursues **delayed gratification**.
    - Mechanism: Inhibitory control of the prefrontal cortex.
    - Goal: Ensure the long-term interests of the group (e.g., farming requires waiting for autumn harvest, saving for the future).

#### 1.2 Domestication Process

Memes domesticate genes by **reconstructing reward circuits**:

1. **Suppression**: Suppressing instinctual impulses through moral shame (Cortisol/stress hormones).
    - *Case*: Fasting, celibacy, asceticism.
2. **Transfer**: Shifting dopamine triggers from "physiological satisfaction" to "social recognition."
    - *Case*: Receiving a medal brings more pleasure than receiving food.
3. **Alienation (Modern Disease)**:
    - Commercial memes (consumerism) reverse-exploit gene instincts through **supernormal stimuli**.
    - *Case*: Short videos, high-sugar foods, pornography. They bypass meme censorship and directly bombard the gene's primitive receptors, leading to **"meme rebellion."**

---

### II. Cultural Variation Mechanism (Conservative Inheritance vs. Radical Innovation)

#### 2.1 Evolutionary Rate Mismatch

- **Gene Evolution**: Extremely slow (ten-thousand-year scale). Tends toward **conservatism**, maintaining physiological homeostasis.
- **Meme Evolution**: Extremely fast (decade scale). Tends toward **radicalism**, trying new strategies.

#### 2.2 The Double-Edged Sword of Variation

1. **Adaptive Innovation**: Meme variation helps genes break through environmental constraints.
    - *Case*: Cold-resistant gene evolution requires tens of thousands of years, but the "sewing fur garments" meme allows tropical naked apes to survive the Ice Age in just one generation.
2. **Adaptive Trap**: Meme variation is too fast, causing genes to be unable to adapt.
    - *Case*: The high-carbohydrate diet of agricultural civilization (meme) led to diabetes and obesity (genes not adapted).
    - *Case*: The atomized life of modern cities (meme) led to widespread loneliness and depression (gregarious genes not adapted).

---

### III. System Immunity Mechanism (Entropy Reduction Maintenance vs. Individual Elimination)

#### 3.1 The System's Low-Entropy Demand

As a dissipative structure, the social system must eliminate internal "chaos factors" to maintain overall order (low entropy). From the system's perspective, **individuals who do not comply with meme domestication are "cancer cells."**

#### 3.2 Core Indicators: Parasitism and Fission

The system identifies "cancer cells" through two major indicators:

1. **Parasitic Coefficient (Pc)**:
    - Definition: Individuals or classes that occupy resources without contributing negative entropy.
    - *Biological Analogy*: Senescent cells that consume energy without working.
    - *Sociological Manifestation*: Rentier classes, slackers, overly bloated bureaucracies.
2. **Fission Index (Sd)**:
    - Definition: The degree to which identification with different meme systems leads to decreased group collaboration efficiency.
    - *Biological Analogy*: Autoimmune diseases (immune system attacks its own cells).
    - *Sociological Manifestation*: Religious conflicts, partisan polarization, separatism.

#### 3.3 Elimination and Discipline

When **System Risk (R) ∝ Pc × Sd** exceeds the threshold, the system initiates an immune response:

- **Mild Measures**: Educational assimilation, public opinion isolation, marginalization.
- **Violent Measures**: Imprisonment, exile, war cleansing.

> So-called "justice" is often the **antibody** of the system's immune response; and so-called "heresy" is often the **antigen** that the system attempts to eliminate.
This statement is a typical application of the **Clairvoyance Analysis Method** in the social system immunity mechanism, aimed at **disenchanting and de-moralizing "justice" and "heresy" through biological analogy**.

* **Social System ≈ Biological Organism**: To maintain its own ordered (low-entropy) survival, the system must identify "one of us" and "enemies."
* **Justice ≈ Antibody**: Not absolute good, but a **tool** manufactured by the system to identify, mark, and eliminate threats.

### **In-Depth Analysis**

#### **1. The Functional Essence of Justice (Antibody)**
In conventional narratives, "justice" represents the sacred, the correct, and the moral high ground.
But from the perspective of **Tiandao Theory**, "justice" is an **immune identification code** evolved by the system.
* **Function**: When a behavior is marked as "unjust," all individuals in the system (white blood cells) are mobilized to attack it.
* **Relativity**: Just as different species have different antibodies, different social systems' "justice" is also completely different, even opposite.
    * *Case*: In Sparta, infanticide was "justice" (for the strength of the population); in modern society, infanticide is "the most heinous crime." The content of justice has changed, but the **function of "maintaining system survival" has not changed**.

#### **2. The Structural Essence of Heresy (Antigen)**
"Heresy" is often not because they are evil, but because they **trigger the system's rejection response**.
* **Type A: Meme Fission (Ideological Antigen)**
    * If an idea (such as heliocentrism) challenges the system's core legitimizing narrative (such as geocentric theocracy), it will cause the system's **Fission Index (Sd)** to soar, and the system must eliminate it like a virus.
* **Type B: Resource Parasitism (Material Antigen)**
    * If a group (such as refugees or monopoly oligarchs) only consumes resources without contributing to order, causing the **Parasitic Coefficient (Pc)** to be too high, the system will attempt to expel or eliminate them.

Humans are not only carriers of genes, but also hosts of memes.
