# 4 Five Major Domestication Systems

Meme domestication is not a single mechanism, but a systematic engineering composed of five major domestication systems. These five systems cooperate with each other to jointly achieve comprehensive control over individuals at different levels of society.

## Overview of Five Major Domestication Systems

| Domestication System | Core Function | Target Group | Typical Memes |
|---------------------|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| Moral Domestication | Reduce governance costs | Bottom layer people | Hard work leads to wealth, suffering is a blessing |
| Love Domestication | Stabilize family structure | Gender groups | True love is unique, marriage is sacred |
| Consumption Domestication | Harvest wealth energy | All groups | Consumption upgrade, identity symbols |
| Success Doctrine Domestication | Maintain system stability | Those desiring bottom-up mobility | Hard work leads to success |
| Collective Domestication | Extract individual energy | All citizens | Patriotism, honor, mission |

## I. Moral Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Reduce governance costs, stabilize bottom layer order. Let the bottom layer willingly be harvested, not resist, not question, not seek class transition.

### Core Mechanism

Moral domestication packages "obedience" as "virtue," defines "resistance" as "evil," making people actively accept the fate of being harvested.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. Hard Work Leads to Wealth

**Surface Meaning**: As long as you work hard, you can obtain wealth

**Actual Function**:
- Make people believe in the rationality of existing distribution system
- Attribute poverty to personal lack of effort, avoiding structural injustice
- Consume bottom layer energy, reduce resistance possibility

**Essence**: Placebo meme defending existing order

#### 2. Suffering is a Blessing

**Surface Meaning**: People willing to suffer will receive blessings

**Actual Function**:
- Make bottom layer actively give up rights
- Suppress individual rights awareness
- Reduce conflict occurrence rate

**Essence**: Self-persuasion mechanism rationalizing exploitation

#### 3. Loyalty and Filial Piety

**Surface Meaning**: Maintain loyalty and filial piety to authority and elders

**Actual Function**:
- Strengthen legitimacy of hierarchy system
- Facilitate elder extraction of younger generation's resources
- Maintain intergenerational oppression structure

**Essence**: Moral packaging of family internal energy upward flow

#### 4. Patience and Non-Contention

**Surface Meaning**: Be patient, don't fight

**Actual Function**:
- Make bottom layer give up fighting for rights
- Maintain existing resource distribution pattern
- Reduce system friction costs

**Essence**: Rhetorical tool reducing governance costs

### Operating Mechanism

```
Moral Narrative Construction
    ↓
"Obedience = Virtue" Concept Internalization
    ↓
When harvested, self-persuasion: "This is what I should bear"
    ↓
Active acceptance rather than resistance
    ↓
Governance costs greatly reduced
```

## II. Love Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Bind genders, stabilize families, ensure offspring rearing. Serve gene continuation and social structure stability.

### Core Mechanism

Love domestication sacralizes "love," stigmatizes "being single," making people actively enter marriage and family structures, undertaking major investment in raising offspring.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. One Life One World One Person

**Surface Meaning**: True love is unique and eternal

**Actual Function**:
- Suppress individual exploration of multiple relationships
- Control sexual partner number to minimum
- Ensure certain parental investment in offspring

**Essence**: Tool reducing infidelity rate, stabilizing spouse relationships

#### 2. Sacrifice for Love

**Surface Meaning**: True love requires giving and sacrifice

**Actual Function**:
- Rationalize one party's resource extraction from another
- Equate "sacrifice" with "proof of love"
- Suppress individual self-interest protection awareness

**Essence**: Moral packaging of unequal energy flow in intimate relationships

#### 3. Marriage is Sacred Responsibility

**Surface Meaning**: Marriage is a sacred life mission

**Actual Function**:
- Urge individuals to enter marriage structure
- Stigmatize single status
- Ensure stability of population reproduction

**Essence**: Infrastructure for social structure stability

### Operating Mechanism

```
Love Meme Implantation
    ↓
Equate "love" with "life meaning"
    ↓
Individuals actively enter monogamous family
    ↓
Undertake huge investment in raising offspring
    ↓
Serve gene replication and social structure stability
```

## III. Consumption Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Stimulate desires, create involution, harvest wealth. Make people work for goods, lifelong debt, their own energy continuously extracted.

### Core Mechanism

Consumption domestication creates false needs, binds human value with commodity consumption, making people constantly pursue more consumption, falling into a desire cycle that can never be satisfied.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. You Deserve Better

**Surface Meaning**: You deserve better goods

**Actual Function**:
- Stimulate pursuit of higher-grade goods
- Rationalize超前consumption
- Continuously drive consumption demand

**Essence**: Marketing tool stimulating consumption desire

#### 2. Loving Yourself Means Buying

**Surface Meaning**: Consumption is a way of self-care

**Actual Function**:
- Bind consumption with self-value
- Use shopping to relieve negative emotions
- Form emotion-consumption compulsive cycle

**Essence**: Domestication mechanism turning people into consumers

#### 3. Taste = Class

**Surface Meaning**: What you consume represents who you are

**Actual Function**:
- Define personal identity with goods
- Stimulate comparative consumption
- Continuously chase higher "class symbols"

**Essence**: Class game maintaining never-satisfied consumption

#### 4. Not Buying is Failure

**Surface Meaning**: Not consuming is shameful

**Actual Function**:
- Create shopping anxiety
- Stigmatize rational consumption
- Compulsive shopping behavior

**Essence**: Shame tool depriving individual rational judgment ability

### Operating Mechanism

```
Consumption Meme Implantation
    ↓
"Consumption = Self-Value" Concept Formation
    ↓
Define personal identity with goods
    ↓
Continuously chase more consumption
    ↓
Wealth continuously harvested
    ↓
Used to repay debt and buy more goods
    ↓
Cycle repeats, lifelong debt
```

## IV. Success Doctrine Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Give bottom layer hope, prevent system collapse. Social trophic level structure determines the vast majority cannot transition, success doctrine is just a placebo meme appeasing the bottom layer.

### Core Mechanism

Success doctrine domestication creates the illusion that "everyone can succeed," making the bottom layer believe they can achieve class transition through personal effort, thus accepting the status quo, not questioning the system itself.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. As Long as You Work Hard You Can Reverse Destiny

**Surface Meaning**: Anyone can succeed as long as they work hard

**Actual Function**:
- Attribute failure to personal lack of effort
- Avoid structural reasons for class solidification
- Make bottom layer continuously involute rather than resist

**Essence**: Placebo maintaining system stability

#### 2. You're Not Successful Because You're Not Self-Disciplined

**Surface Meaning**: Failure is due to personal quality issues

**Actual Function**:
- Attribute structural dilemmas to personal qualities
- Trigger self-condemnation rather than system questioning
- Continuously consume individual energy in involution

**Essence**: Psychological mechanism shifting system responsibility

#### 3. Poor Families Produce Noble Sons

**Surface Meaning**: Poor families can also produce talents

**Actual Function**:
- Use extremely few reverse destiny cases to cover universal patterns
- Make people believe class transition is universally possible
- Ignore statistically extremely low probability

**Essence**: Survivor bias defending unequal structures

### Operating Mechanism

```
Success Doctrine Meme Implantation
    ↓
"I can also succeed" Illusion Formation
    ↓
Bottom layer continuously involutes, self-improves
    ↓
Vast majority cannot transition, but still believe "I'm not working hard enough"
    ↓
Don't question the system itself
    ↓
System stability maintained
```

## V. Collective Domestication Memes

### Core Function

Drive individuals to sacrifice for the group, maintain super group competitive advantage. Maximally extract individual energy to the group, is the most efficient energy harvesting mechanism.

### Core Mechanism

Collective domestication sacralizes "collective interest," making individuals willingly contribute their own energy to the collective, achieving highly concentrated and unified allocation of energy.

### Typical Meme Analysis

#### 1. Patriotism

**Surface Meaning**: Love your country

**Actual Function**:
- Place national interest above personal interest
- Rationalize individual sacrifice for country
- Stimulate hostility and competition toward out-groups

**Essence**: Emotional tool for super group cohesion

#### 2. Collective Honor

**Surface Meaning**: Collective honor is above personal interest

**Actual Function**:
- Make individuals sacrifice for collective honor
- Drive behavior with sense of honor
- Suppress individual interest demands

**Essence**: Honor packaging of energy upward flow

#### 3. Mission Calling

**Surface Meaning**: Everyone has a special mission

**Actual Function**:
- Give individuals a sacred purpose in life
- Make people actively pursue "mission"
- Drive behavior with sense of mission

**Essence**: Sacralized narrative of individual energy being extracted

#### 4. Sacrifice Small Self to Complete Big Self

**Surface Meaning**: For bigger goals, sacrifice the individual

**Actual Function**:
- Sacralize individual sacrifice
- Rationalize resource extraction from individuals
- Suppress individual self-protection awareness

**Essence**: Most efficient energy harvesting rhetoric

### Operating Mechanism

```
Collective Meme Implantation
    ↓
"Collective Interest Above Personal" Concept Internalization
    ↓
Individuals actively contribute energy to collective
    ↓
Energy flows upward and concentrates
    ↓
Top layer controls energy allocation
    ↓
Super group competitive ability strengthened
```

## Practical Application

### Identifying Five Major Domestication Systems

1. **Moral Domestication**: Are you using "should/shouldn't" to suppress rational judgment?
2. **Love Domestication**: Is being single stigmatized? Is "true love" used to rationalize unequal relationships?
3. **Consumption Domestication**: Are you using consumption to define self-value? Are you trapped in a never-satisfied shopping cycle?
4. **Success Doctrine Domestication**: Are you attributing failure to personal? Are you avoiding structural injustice?
5. **Collective Domestication**: Are you placing collective interest above personal interest? Have you sacrificed personal development for the collective?

### Principles of Resisting Domestication

1. **Moral Independence**: Don't be kidnapped by "should/shouldn't," use reason to judge behavior consequences
2. **Emotional Autonomy**: Don't be defined by "true love," independently decide intimate relationship forms
3. **Consumption Rationality**: Don't be defined by goods for self-value, spend within means
4. **Structural Cognition**: See structural reasons for class solidification, don't attribute failure to personal
5. **Individual Value**: Recognize individual value doesn't depend on collective, rationally evaluate collective interest
