# 2 Micro-Foundation: Two Core Cornerstones

## Core Proposition

The micro-foundation layer is the foundation of this theory, explaining the underlying logic of "where humans come from and how society operates." It consists of two core cornerstones: **Gene Group Evolution Theory** (First Cornerstone) and **Meme Evolution and Domestication Theory** (Second Cornerstone).

The relationship between these two cornerstones: **Gene Group Evolution Theory** answers "where humans come from and why they are like this," while **Meme Evolution and Domestication Theory** answers "how society controls people and how individuals are domesticated." The two progress layer by layer, together forming a complete logical chain from biological instincts to social control.

## Positioning of Two Cornerstones

### First Cornerstone: Gene Group Evolution Theory

**Core Question**: Where do humans come from? Why are they like this?

**Theoretical Position**: Explaining biological instincts and group evolution dynamics

**Core Content**:
- The only underlying goal of life: survival and replication
- The unit of evolution is not the individual, but the gene group
- Three core mechanisms: kin selection, group selection, reciprocal altruism

### Second Cornerstone: Meme Evolution and Domestication Theory

**Core Question**: How does society control people? How are individuals domesticated?

**Theoretical Position**: Explaining the spiritual control mechanism of cultural memes on humans, is the bridge connecting the biological bottom layer with social structure, and is also the logical starting point of the entire theory's "anti-domestication, gaining cognitive sovereignty"

**Core Content**:
- Three Laws of Meme Evolution: transmission priority, meme complex formation, self-protection mechanism
- Three-Layer Domestication Structure: emotional anchor → identity → meaning assignment
- Five Major Domestication Systems: morality, love, consumption, success doctrine, collectivism
- Anti-domestication practice: Lingshi theory

## Theoretical Framework

### I. Dual Coding Essence Axiom

Humans are a life continuation system jointly constituted by genes and cultural memes, co-evolving:
- **Gene Layer**: Sets underlying biological instincts (five desires), provides the system's energy supply and collaboration prototype
- **Meme Layer**: Constructs super-biological adaptation systems (rules, institutions, culture), expands survival strategy space
- **Collaborative Relationship**: Genes provide the material basis for meme evolution, memes reversely shape gene expression

### II. Group Evolution Priority Axiom

In long-term evolution, the survival weight of the group is higher than the survival weight of the individual. Individual behaviors that cannot maintain group survival will eventually be eliminated by evolution.

### III. Meme Domestication Mechanism Axiom

Social systems achieve coordination of individual behaviors through non-violent means, and the internalization of social norms makes individual behaviors consciously coordinated.

### IV. Cognitive Breakthrough Theorem

The cognitive layer is the highest dimension of evolution, and individuals can achieve anti-domestication of consciousness and cognitive sovereignty through cognitive awakening.

### V. Energy Conversion Theorem

The stability of social systems depends on the effective conversion of individual energy into system energy, and false subjectivity is the key mechanism to achieve this conversion.

### VI. Group Selection Theorem

Characteristics beneficial to group survival will be retained and strengthened, and the evolution of altruistic behavior and the formation of group cohesion are the results of group selection.

### VII. Super Group Competition Theorem

Competition between different civilizations and countries is essentially the survival game of gene-culture complexes, evolving from low-dimensional zero-sum games to high-dimensional non-zero-sum games.

### VIII. Structure Emergence Principle

Social structure is the adapter of gene groups, individuals must adapt to the structure to survive, and violating the structure will result in punishment.

## Theoretical Significance

The micro-foundation layer provides a unified theoretical framework for understanding the evolution of human society. It integrates research results from multiple disciplines such as biology, sociology, and cultural studies into one system, providing new perspectives and methods for explaining various phenomena in human society.

## Practical Application

The theoretical results of the micro-foundation layer can be applied to multiple fields such as social governance, cultural construction, and educational reform, providing theoretical guidance and practical paths for solving practical problems, especially providing systematic methodological support for "anti-domestication, gaining cognitive sovereignty."
