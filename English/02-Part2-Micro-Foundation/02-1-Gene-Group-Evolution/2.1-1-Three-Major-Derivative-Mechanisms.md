# 3 Human Nature Essence: Gene-Meme Dual Coding

## 3.4 Derivative Mechanisms of Dual Coding: Instincts, Domestication, and Games

### Core Proposition

**The interaction between genes (instincts) and memes (culture) is not always harmonious symbiosis, but a continuous dynamic game within the individual body. This game has derived three core mechanisms of human society: desire domestication, cultural variation, and system immunity.**

---

### I. Desire Domestication Mechanism (Immediate Feedback vs Delayed Gratification)

#### 1.1 Conflict Source

- **Gene Logic (Instinct)**: Pursues **immediate feedback**.
    - Mechanism: Dopamine reward circuit.
    - Goal: Ensure immediate survival and reproduction (e.g., eat sugar when seeing it, chase opposite sex when seeing them).
- **Meme Logic (Culture)**: Pursues **delayed gratification**.
    - Mechanism: Inhibitory control of the prefrontal cortex.
    - Goal: Ensure the long-term interests of the group (e.g., farming requires waiting for autumn harvest, saving for the future).

#### 1.2 Domestication Process

Memes domesticate genes by **reconstructing reward circuits**:

1.  **Suppression**: Inhibit instinctive impulses through moral shame (Cortisol/stress hormone).
    - *Case*: Fasting, celibacy, asceticism.
2.  **Transfer**: Transfer dopamine's trigger point from "physiological satisfaction" to "social recognition."
    - *Case*: Getting a medal brings more pleasure than getting food.
3.  **Alienation (Modern Disease)**:
    - Commercial memes (consumerism) reversely exploit gene instincts through **supernormal stimuli**.
    - *Case*: Short videos, high-sugar foods, pornography industry. They bypass meme censorship and directly bombard gene's primitive receptors, leading to **"meme rebellion"**.

---

### II. Cultural Variation Mechanism (Conservative Inheritance vs Radical Innovation)

#### 2.1 Evolution Rate Mismatch

- **Gene Evolution**: Extremely slow (ten thousand year scale). Tends toward **conservatism**, maintaining physiological homeostasis.
- **Meme Evolution**: Extremely fast (decade scale). Tends toward **radicalism**, trying new strategies.

#### 2.2 Double-Edged Sword of Variation

1.  **Adaptive Innovation**: Meme variation helps genes break through environmental limits.
    - *Case*: Cold-resistant gene evolution takes tens of thousands of years, but the "sewing fur" meme takes only one generation to let tropical naked apes survive the ice age.
2.  **Adaptive Trap**: Meme variation is too fast, causing genes to be unable to adapt.
    - *Case*: High-carbohydrate diet brought by agricultural civilization (meme) caused diabetes and obesity (genes cannot adapt).
    - *Case*: Atomized life in modern cities (meme) caused widespread loneliness and depression (social genes cannot adapt).

---

### III. System Immunity Mechanism (Entropy Reduction Maintenance vs Individual Clearance)

#### 3.1 System's Low Entropy Demand

As a dissipative structure, the social system must clear internal "chaos factors" to maintain overall order (low entropy). From the system's perspective, **individuals who do not obey meme domestication are "cancer cells"**.

#### 3.2 Core Indicators: Parasitism and Division

The system identifies "cancer cells" through two major indicators:

1.  **Parasitism Coefficient (Pc)**:
    - Definition: Individuals or classes that occupy resources but do not contribute negative entropy.
    - *Biological Analogy*: Senescent cells that consume energy but do not work.
    - *Sociological Manifestation*: Rentier class, lazy people, overly bloated bureaucracy.
2.  **Division Index (Sd)**:
    - Definition: The degree to which identifying with different meme systems leads to decreased group collaboration efficiency.
    - *Biological Analogy*: Autoimmune diseases (immune system attacks own cells).
    - *Sociological Manifestation*: Religious conflicts, partisan polarization, separatism.

#### 3.3 Clearance and Discipline

When **System Risk (R) ∝ Pc × Sd** exceeds the threshold, the system initiates immune response:

- **Mild Means**: Educational assimilation, public opinion isolation, marginalization.
- **Violent Means**: Imprisonment, exile, war cleansing.

**Lingshi Insight**:
> So-called "justice" is often the **antibody** of the system's immune response; and so-called "heresy" is often the **antigen** that the system tries to clear.

This statement is a typical application of **Lingshi analysis method** in the social system immune mechanism, aiming to **de-mystify and de-moralize "justice" and "heresy" through biological analogy**.

### **Core Metaphor**

*   **Social System ≈ Biological Organism**: To maintain its own ordered (low entropy) existence, the system must identify "self" and "enemy."
*   **Justice ≈ Antibody**: Not absolute good, but a **tool** manufactured by the system to identify, mark, and clear threats.
*   **Heresy ≈ Antigen**: Not absolute evil, but any **incompatible** foreign object that may cause system division (Sd increase) or resource loss (Pc increase).

### **Deep Analysis**

#### **1. Functional Essence of Justice (Antibody)**
In conventional narratives, "justice" represents sacred, correct, and moral high ground.
But from the perspective of **TianDao Theory**, "justice" is the **immune identification code** evolved by the system.
*   **Function**: When a behavior is marked as "unjust," all individuals in the system (white blood cells) are mobilized to attack it.
*   **Relativity**: Just as different species have different antibodies, different social systems' "justice" is also completely different, even opposite.
    *   *Case*: In Sparta, infanticide was "justice" (for population strength); in modern society, infanticide is "heinous crime." The content of justice changed, but the **function of "maintaining system survival" did not change**.

#### **2. Structural Essence of Heresy (Antigen)**
"Heresy" is often not because they are evil, but because they **triggered the system's rejection reaction**.
*   **Type A: Meme Division (Ideological Antigen)**
    *   If an idea (like heliocentrism) challenges the system's core legitimacy narrative (like geocentrism theocracy), it will cause the system's **Division Index (Sd)** to soar, and the system must clear it like a virus.
*   **Type B: Resource Parasitism (Material Antigen)**
    *   If a group (like refugees or monopoly oligarchs) only consumes resources without contributing to order, causing **Parasitism Coefficient (Pc)** to be too high, the system will try to expel or eliminate them.
