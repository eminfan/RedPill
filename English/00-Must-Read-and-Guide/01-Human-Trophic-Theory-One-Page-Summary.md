# The Human Trophic Theory: The Underlying Logic of Human Social Evolution (One-Page Summary)

> **Core Axiom**: The universe tends toward entropy increase (disorder), and life is a dissipative structure that reverses entropy (order). All systems, cultures, and conflicts in human society are essentially about **competing for negative entropy (resources/energy)** to maintain survival order.

---

### I. Microscopic Mechanism: What is a Human?
**Conclusion: Humans are dual slaves of "genes" and "memes."**
1.  **Genetic Coding (Biological Instincts)**: Drives you to survive, reproduce, and pursue pleasure.
2.  **Memetic Coding (Cultural Programs)**: "Software" implanted in your brain through language, education, and media.
    *   **Memetic Domestication**: Society uses concepts like "morality," "ideals," and "success" to convert your biological energy into group order (e.g., using "patriotism" to make individuals sacrifice, using "consumerism" to make individuals labor).
    *   **False Subjectivity**: You think you are thinking, but actually memes are thinking through your brain.

### II. Mesoscopic Structure: What is Society?
**Conclusion: Society is a "pyramid" ecosystem of energy flow.**
Social structure is not artificially designed but **emerges** from the games of countless individuals into three **trophic levels**:
1.  **Production Layer (Bottom)**: Directly creates value but is stripped of surplus value (e.g., farmers, workers).
2.  **Transmission Layer (Middle)**: Executes rules, maintains order, intercepts some resources (e.g., bureaucrats, managers).
3.  **Accumulation Layer (Top)**: Makes rules, legally accumulates resources through institutional design (e.g., capitalists, ruling class).
*   **Energy Flow Direction**: Resources naturally flow from the production layer to the accumulation layer, just as energy flows unidirectionally in a food chain.

### III. Macroscopic Laws: Why Does History Cycle?
**Conclusion: Lower Order Tao = Historical Cycle Law (Inevitability of Entropy Increase).**
1.  **Establishment Period**: New institutions reduce social entropy (disorder), releasing productivity.
2.  **Accumulation Period**: The accumulation layer uses rule advantages to continuously absorb bottom-layer resources (land concentration/wealth polarization).
3.  **Rigidity Period**: The accumulation layer forms interest groups that block reform; bottom-layer energy is exhausted, system entropy (resentment/chaos) surges.
4.  **Collapse Period**: The system cannot maintain order, undergoes violent dissipation (war/revolution), energy is redistributed, entering the next cycle.

### IV. Ultimate Way Out: Higher Order Tao
**Conclusion: Break the cycle, achieve civilization upgrade.**
1.  **Cognitive Awakening (Lingshi)**: See through memetic domestication, identify the truth of energy predation behind surface narratives (e.g., "for your own good").
2.  **Structural Reconstruction**: Shift from "predatory structure" (one-way extraction) to **"symbiotic structure"** (two-way nourishment, e.g., open source communities, cooperatives).
3.  **Resisting Entropy Increase**: Continuously introduce negative entropy through technological innovation (growing the pie) and institutional justice (distributing the pie well) to avoid system heat death.
