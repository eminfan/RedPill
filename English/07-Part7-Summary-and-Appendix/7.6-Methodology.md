# Methodology

This project adopts a unique **"physics-biology-society" interdisciplinary reductionism** method, whose core lies in reducing human social phenomena to underlying physical and biological laws, and drawing conclusions through strict logical deduction.

This methodology is called **"Tao Analysis Method"**, its operating mechanism is as follows:

### I. Core Research Methods

This project's research method can be summarized as the dual path of **"axiomatic deduction + Ling-Shi deconstruction"**:

#### 1. Axiomatic Deduction
This is the cornerstone of theory construction. The project does not rely on traditional sociological observations or questionnaire surveys, but starts from **unshakable natural science axioms** for logical derivation.

*   **Meta-Starting Point**: Based on **second law of thermodynamics (entropy increase)** and **evolutionary biology**.
    *   **Axiom 1: Entropy increase is irreversible** — The universe overall tends toward disorder; survival must continuously obtain energy (negative entropy).
    *   **Axiom 2: Resource limitation** — Negative entropy flow (resources) is limited under specific time-space, leading to zero-sum games.
    *   **Axiom 3: Evolutionary selection** — Only structures that can effectively obtain resources and reproduce can survive.
*   **Deductive Logic**:
    *   Because survival needs negative entropy → Individuals must compete for resources.
    *   Because individual power is limited → Must form groups (collaboration value-added).
    *   Because collaboration needs rules → "Memes" (culture/institutions) emerge to constrain instincts.
    *   Because human nature seeks profit and avoids harm → Those mastering rules inevitably tend to prey (social stratification).
    *   **Conclusion**: Social classes, moral laws, state machines are not human-designed, but **inevitable mathematical solutions** of system evolution.

#### 2. Ling-Shi Framework
This is a **"cognitive deconstruction tool"** used to analyze specific social phenomena, enforced in the project. It requires that when analyzing any phenomenon, one must strip away surface moral narratives, reaching directly to underlying interest structures.

*   **Structural Deconstruction**: Decomposing social phenomena into **production layer, transmission layer, enrichment layer** (social trophic level theory).
*   **Energy Tracking (Follow the Energy)**: Not watching people "say what" (surface narrative), only watching where resources/energy "flow to."
*   **Semantic De-anchoring**: Questioning and stripping the hypnotic effect of vocabulary like "for your own good," "for the big picture," restoring their essence as **"meme domestication tools"**.
*   **Incentive Backward Deduction**: Through "who ultimately benefits" backward deducing the true intention of institutional design.

### II. How to Acquire Knowledge and Draw Conclusions

This project acquires knowledge and verifies conclusions through the following **closed-loop process**:

1.  **Establish Axiom System (Origin)**
    *   Establish physics (thermodynamics) and biology (evolution theory) as unquestionable meta-starting points.
    *   Reject any a priori assumptions like "natural human rights" or "human nature is inherently good."

2.  **Model Construction (Modeling)**
    *   Construct **"gene-meme dual coding"** model: Humans are hosts for genes (biological instincts) and memes (cultural software).
    *   Construct **"social trophic level"** model: Society is an energy-flowing pyramid structure.

3.  **Logical Deduction (Deduction)**
    *   Under resource-limited premises, deduce inevitable patterns of group behavior.
    *   For example: Deriving that **"historical cycle law"** is not accident, but inevitable physical process of dissipative structure collapse caused by system entropy accumulation.

4.  **Empirical Verification (Verification)**
    *   Use **historical cases** (like Qing Dynasty rule, Japanese militarism) and **contemporary phenomena** (like American happy education, Soviet planned economy) for verification.
    *   Verification standard: Does the "energy flow direction" derived from theory match historical facts?
    *   **Case**: When analyzing "happy education," not looking at its "respecting nature" propaganda, but looking at its results — public education through "burden reduction" reduced bottom layer's ability to obtain quality educational resources, thereby solidifying classes (energy flowing to enrichment layer), verifying "class solidification theorem."

5.  **Conclusion Generation (Conclusion)**
    *   Conclusions are often **counter-intuitive** and **de-moralized**.
    *   For example: Morality is not sacred truth, but **"algorithms"** reducing internal collaboration costs of groups; the state is not a community, but a tool for class rule (under lower-order Tao).

### Summary
This project's research method is **"examining human society with physics' cold gaze"**. It doesn't ask "what the world should be" (ought), only asks "what the world inevitably is under physical and biological laws" (is). Through **axiomatic deduction** ensuring logical rigor, through **Ling-Shi deconstruction** ensuring penetration of reality.
