# Appendix B: Axiom System Summary

## I. Meta-Axioms (Universe and Life Underlying Laws)

### Axiom 1: Entropy Increase Background Axiom
**The universe overall tends toward disorder; life as an ordered structure must continuously obtain resources from outside to maintain survival, this is the foundation of all life behavior.**
- **Physical Basis**: Second law of thermodynamics
- **Social Significance**: Social systems as dissipative structures require continuous resource supply

### Axiom 2: Resource Time-Space Limitation Axiom
**Within specific time and space, natural resources directly usable by humans have upper limits; technology can continuously expand resource boundaries but cannot break through instantaneous time-space limits.**
- **Economic Significance**: Resource scarcity is the fundamental driver of social distribution games
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Resource competition is the core driver of group evolution

### Axiom 3: Group Evolution Priority Axiom
**The core unit of life evolution is the gene group (sharing blood foundation and cultural rules); the group's long-term survival and security has priority over individual short-term interests.**
- **Biological Basis**: Group selection theory
- **Social Significance**: The ultimate driver of individual behavior is gene group survival

### Axiom 4: Dual Heredity System Axiom
**Humans rely on two systems for inheritance: genes determine physiological instincts (evolves very slowly); memes (institutions, technology, morality, concepts) determine social rules (evolves very fast), memes directly determine group fate.**
- **Biological Significance**: Gene-meme co-evolution
- **Social Significance**: Cultural memes are the operating system of social systems

## II. Social Evolution Axioms (Human Society Specific Laws)

### Axiom 5: Collaboration Value-Added Axiom
**Humans through division of labor, collaboration, innovation can create incremental resources far greater than simple addition of individuals; society is not just about stock competition.**
- **Economic Significance**: Division of labor collaboration improves production efficiency
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Collaboration is the source of group advantage

### Axiom 6: Predatory Profit-Seeking Axiom
**In spontaneous order without global constraints, some individuals will tend to use monopoly resources, control power to uncompensatedly occupy others' labor results, because predation cost is lower than direct production.**
- **Behavioral Basis**: Rational profit-seeking principle
- **Social Significance**: Predation is inevitable tendency of spontaneous order

### Axiom 7: Meme Legitimization Axiom
**Groups mastering rule-making power will use morality, institutions, ideology to package their predatory behavior as reasonable, inevitable, legitimate rules, reducing governance and stability maintenance costs.**
- **Sociological Significance**: Ideology's social control function
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Meme domestication is necessary mechanism for system stability

## III. Social Trophic Level Theory Axioms

### Axiom 8: Dissipative Structure Survival Axiom
**Human society is a typical dissipative system, must continuously obtain survival development resources from outside, through resource consumption offset system's internal spontaneous disordering trend.**
- **Physical Basis**: Second law of thermodynamics
- **Social Significance**: Resource acquisition and efficient utilization is the first prerequisite for social survival

### Axiom 9: Resource Scarcity Axiom
**Within specific time-space closed systems, total survival development resources available to society have rigid upper limits.**
- **Economic Basis**: Resource scarcity is basic assumption of economics
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Resource competition is fundamental driver of social stratification

### Axiom 10: Rational Profit-Seeking Axiom
**Without global rigid institutional constraints, individuals/groups with resource and power advantages will prioritize "minimum cost maximum benefit" behavior patterns.**
- **Behavioral Basis**: Rational person assumption
- **Social Significance**: Predation is inevitable tendency of spontaneous order

### Axiom 11: Surplus Resource Natural Enrichment Axiom
**The only original source of social survival development resources is bottom production group's labor creation; in spontaneous state without external rigid constraints, surplus resources naturally have structural tendency flowing from "production end" to "rule-making end."**
- **Economic Basis**: Surplus value theory
- **Social Significance**: Resource flow directionality is direct cause of social stratification

## IV. Gene Group Evolution Theory Axioms

### Axiom 12: Dual Coding Essence Axiom
**Humans are life continuation systems jointly constituted and co-evolving with genes and cultural memes.**
- **Gene Layer**: Sets underlying biological instincts (five desires), provides system's energy supply and collaboration prototype
- **Meme Layer**: Constructs super-biological adaptation systems (rules, institutions, culture), expands survival strategy space
- **Collaborative Relationship**: Genes provide material basis for meme evolution, memes reversely shape gene expression

### Axiom 13: Extended Phenotype Principle
**Social systems are "extended bodies" constructed by gene groups in the cultural layer to maximize their own fitness.**
- **Biological Origin**: Proposed by Dawkins in "The Selfish Gene"
- **Social Significance**: Social systems are gene groups' collective adaptors

### Axiom 14: Negative Entropy Drive Logic
**Social system's stability (low entropy) depends on continuously consuming individual economic, physiological, psychological, information energy.**
- **Thermodynamic Basis**: Dissipative structure theory
- **Social Significance**: System maintaining order requires energy input

### Axiom 15: Meme Domestication Mechanism
**Systems through meme domestication transform discrete individual energy into group entropy reduction (order).**
- **Core Mechanism**: Selective redundancy, semantic anchoring, false subjectivity
- **Social Significance**: Deep mechanism of non-violent social control

### Axiom 16: Dynasty Cycle Law Fate
**Stability-maintenance type systems follow the dynamic fate of "formation (entropy decrease) → stability (low entropy) → maturity (lowest entropy) → rigidity (entropy increase) → collapse (entropy explosion)."**
- **System Dynamics**: Entropy change patterns
- **Historical Significance**: Explains underlying logic of dynasty rise and fall

### Axiom 17: Infinite Evolution Conditions
**Breaking through cycle law must simultaneously satisfy five conditions: infinite energy flow, information openness, meme updating, power dispersion, self-repair.**
- **Systems Science**: Complex adaptive systems theory
- **Future Significance**: Necessary conditions for civilization continuous evolution
