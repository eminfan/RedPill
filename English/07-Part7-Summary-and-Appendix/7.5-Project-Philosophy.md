# Project Philosophy

This project believes in a **dialectical unity of cold physicalism, thorough evolutionism, and existentialism**.

If summarized in one sentence, its philosophical core is: **"In the nihilistic universe of entropy increase, through cognitive awakening and anti-entropy work, construct human subjectivity and civilization's order."**

Specifically, it can be interpreted from the following four dimensions:

### 1. Ontology: Physicalism and Dissipative Structure (What is the world?)
This project rejects all mysticism and a priori morality, insisting on **"physical laws supreme"**.
*   **World Essence**: The universe's base color is **entropy increase** (moving toward disorder and heat death).
*   **Life Essence**: Life and civilization are **"dissipative structures"**, must continuously ingest **negative entropy** (energy/resources) from outside to resist their own disintegration.
*   **Survival Iron Law**: Survival is the first primary drive; all morality, culture, institutions are tools evolved to serve "gene group survival."

### 2. Epistemology: Structural Realism and Cognitive Deconstruction (How to see the world?)
This project is extremely skeptical of surface narratives, advocating **"penetrating appearances, reaching structure directly"**.
*   **Anti-Domestication**: Believes mainstream morality, law, ideology (like "loyal to ruler and country," "consumerism") are often **"memes"**, "software" used by systems to domesticate individuals, reduce governance costs.
*   **Ling-Shi (Spiritual Vision)**: Advocates using scalpel-like reason (structural deconstruction, energy tracking) to dissect society, not listening to "what is said," only watching "where resources flow."
*   **Disenchantment**: Reducing sacred concepts to interest calculations, reducing warm veils to cold games.

### 3. Historical View: From Fatalistic Cycle to Active Evolution (Where is history going?)
*   **Lower-Order Tao (Fatalism)**: Admits that in natural (non-intervention) states, greed in human nature (predatory profit-seeking) inevitably leads society toward **historical cycle law** of "class solidification—system imbalance—collapse reconstruction." This is thermodynamic inevitability.
*   **Higher-Order Tao (Evolutionism)**: Believes civilization can actively resist instinct through rational **"anti-entropy design"** (institutional checks and balances, symbiosis structure), break cycle law, achieve civilization leap. This is a **"defying heaven" rational optimism**.

### 4. Life View: Void Boat Thinking and Cognitive Sovereignty (How should humans live?)
After seeing through the cruel truth of the world (prisoners of genes and memes), this project does not lead to negative nihilism, but advocates **heroic awakening**.
*   **Cognitive Sovereignty**: Life's ultimate goal is to reclaim control of one's own brain, no longer being "meat machines" programmed by memes.
*   **Void Boat Thinking**: Originating from Zhuangzi, advocating **"external transformation but internal non-transformation"**. Behaviorally conforming to social rules (low-energy survival), but spiritually maintaining absolute independence and clarity.
*   **Reconstructing Meaning**: Meaning is not externally given, but self-constructed by the awakened on ruins.

---

### Summary
This project's philosophical base color is **gray (cruel reality)**, but points toward **golden (civilization's dimensional upgrade)**.

It's like the red pill in "The Matrix":
**First making you painfully see the "real wasteland" (physics and evolution's truth), then endowing you with the ability to rewrite code (cognitive awakening and institutional design).**
