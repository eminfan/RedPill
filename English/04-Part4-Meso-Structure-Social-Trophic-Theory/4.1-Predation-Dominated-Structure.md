# 4 Predation-Dominated Structure: The Gravity of Entropy Increase

## 9.1 Core Definition: The System's Default Option

**Predation-dominated structure** is not some evil design, but the **default physical form of human society in its natural state**.

According to the **Energy Minimum Path Axiom**: Without reverse work (institutional constraints), the system always tends to slide toward the state of maximum entropy increase—that is, obtaining energy through **predation** rather than production.

Just like water flows downward, without pumps (higher-order Tao), resources inevitably flow to those with violence and rule-making power.

---

## 9.2 Operating Mechanism: A Precision Water Pump

Predation-type structure is essentially a **"negative entropy water pump."** Through three subsystems, it continuously extracts survival resources from the bottom layer to the top layer.

### 1. Violence Pump (Basic Layer)
- **Principle**: Direct use of coercive force (army, police, prisons) to ensure unidirectional flow of resources.
- **Function**: Ensure the bottom layer dares not cut off energy flow through violent resistance.
- **Energy Consumption**: High. If relying solely on violence, ruling costs are extremely high (Qin Dynasty mode), system extremely unstable.

### 2. Institutional Pump (Middle Layer)
- **Principle**: Through laws, taxes, financial rules, make predation **legalized** and **concealed**.
- **Common Forms**:
    - **Land annexation/rent**: Possess means of production, appropriate surplus value without compensation.
    - **Financial seigniorage**: Dilute bottom layer wealth through inflation.
    - **Franchise rights**: Obtain excess profits through administrative monopoly (salt and iron monopoly).
- **Function**: Reduce the friction coefficient of predation, make the preyed think "that's how rules are."

### 3. Meme Pump (Top Layer)
- **Principle**: Through culture, religion, education, implant **"obedience memes."**
- **Common Narratives**:
    - "Divine right of kings" / "Mandate of Heaven"
    - "Endure hardship to become superior"
    - "Your poverty is because you didn't work hard"
- **Function**: Make batteries fall in love with the battery compartment. This is the highest level of predation, lowest energy consumption, most lasting effect.

## 9.3 System Stabilizer: Placebo Channel

To prevent bottom layer energy from being pumped dry leading to system explosion (revolution), predation-type structures design ingenious **pressure relief valves**:

### 1. Extremely Low Probability Upward Channels (Imperial Examination/Lottery)
- **Mechanism**: Allow extremely few bottom layer individuals (0.01%) to become top layer through imperial examination, college entrance exam, entrepreneurship.
- **Functions**:
    - **Extract Elites**: Absorb the smartest, most capable of resistance individuals from the bottom layer into the ruling group.
    - **Create Hope**: Make the remaining 99.99% think "I didn't succeed because I don't have ability," not "the system has problems."

### 2. Tittytainment
- **Mechanism**: Provide cheap entertainment (gladiatorial arenas, short videos, soap operas).
- **Function**: Consume bottom layer's excess energy (physiological energy) and emotions (psychological energy), prevent them from transforming into thinking and resistance.

## 9.4 Evolutionary Fate: Thermodynamic Collapse

Despite designing multiple stabilization mechanisms, predation-type structures still cannot escape the **"historical cycle law."** This is not because emperors are foolish, but because of **physical laws**.

### Collapse Process

1.  **Early Stage (Establishment)**: New regime resets resource distribution through violence (kill the rich to help the poor), parasitic coefficient $Pc$ low, system rapidly accumulates negative entropy through "rest and recuperation."
2.  **Middle Stage (Enrichment)**: Enrichment layer uses rule advantages to continuously increase pumping intensity. Transmission layer (bureaucrats) begins to expand, intercepting energy. $Pc$ gradually rises.
3.  **Late Stage (Rigidity)**:
    - Top layer annexes vast majority of land/capital.
    - Middle layer intercepts vast majority of taxes.
    - Production layer (peasants/workers) energy input < minimum consumption for survival.
4.  **End Stage (Heat Death/Explosion)**:
    - **Heat Death**: If bottom layer obeys, system enters rigid death state, population plummets, civilization stagnates (like Wei-Jin-Northern and Southern Dynasties).
    - **Explosion**: If bottom layer resists, system undergoes violent dissipation (peasant uprisings), energy reshuffles.

### Conclusion
**Predation-dominated structure has no future.**
It either cycles in periodic collapse, or rots in long-term rigid death.
Unless—introducing **anti-entropy work**, leaping to higher-order Tao.
