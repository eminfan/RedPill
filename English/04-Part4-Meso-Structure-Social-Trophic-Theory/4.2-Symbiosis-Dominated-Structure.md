# 4 Symbiosis-Dominated Structure

## 10.1 Core Logic: Actively Designed Sustainable Form

### Core Proposition

**Symbiosis-dominated structure is a sustainable development structure actively designed and constructed by humans after cognizing the objective laws of social operation, with the core goal of maximizing the long-term survival and evolutionary development of the group as a whole.**

### Design Principles

1. **Systemic Principle**: Design structure from the perspective of the overall system, not local optimization
2. **Fairness Principle**: Ensure opportunity fairness in resource distribution, eliminate institutionalized predation
3. **Efficiency Principle**: Improve resource utilization efficiency through reasonable division of labor and collaboration
4. **Sustainability Principle**: Ensure long-term stability and evolutionary capability of the system
5. **Adaptability Principle**: Ability to self-adjust and optimize with environmental changes

### Operating Logic

- **Resource Two-Way Circulation**: Surplus resources form positive circulation within the system, feeding back to the production layer
- **Open and Transparent Rules**: Resource distribution rules are open and transparent, accepting supervision
- **Power Checks and Balances**: Achieve effective checks and balances of power through institutional design
- **Innovation Incentives**: Establish mechanisms encouraging innovation, release individual vitality

## 10.2 Key Characteristics: Anti-Predation and Positive Resource Circulation

### 1. Zero Institutionalized Predation

- **Core Characteristic**: Ownership structure with public ownership of means of production as the main body and joint development of multiple ownership economies as the core, fundamentally abolishing uncompensated predation based on means of production and power monopoly
- **Implementation Methods**:
  - Property rights system reform: Clarify public ownership or fair distribution of means of production
  - Distribution system design: Implement distribution according to work as the main body, coexisting with multiple distribution methods
  - Supervision mechanism: Build a diversified rigid supervision system, regulate power operation
- **Result**: Individual/group income only linked to labor contribution, management value, technological innovation, risk bearing, completely closing the institutional channel of getting something for nothing

### 2. Two-Way Positive Circulation of Resource Flow

- **Core Characteristic**: Surplus resources created by the production layer no longer unidirectionally enrich toward the top layer, but return to the public domain through public distribution systems
- **Flow Path**:
  - Production layer creates surplus resources
  - Part of surplus resources used for public undertakings (education, healthcare, infrastructure, innovation)
  - Public undertakings feed back to production layer, improve production efficiency
  - Form positive closed loop of "production-distribution-feedback-reproduction"
- **Result**: All members share development results, system achieves sustainable development

### 3. Opportunity Fairness in Class Mobility

- **Core Characteristic**: The only standard for individual level advancement is labor contribution, ability level and innovation results, completely unrelated to background, intergenerational resources, blood background
- **Implementation Methods**:
  - Education equalization: Ensure fair distribution of educational resources
  - Employment fairness: Eliminate employment discrimination, establish fair selection mechanisms
  - Social security: Establish social assistance and bottom-line mechanisms
  - Entrepreneurship support: Provide entrepreneurship opportunities and resources for bottom layer groups
- **Result**: Class mobility smooth, social vitality fully released

### 4. Service-Oriented Reconstruction of Level Functions

- **Core Characteristic**: Core functions of enrichment layer and transmission layer completely transform from "rule-making and resource predation" to "serving group development, bearing system risks"
- **Implementation Methods**:
  - Role repositioning: Clarify service functions of each level
  - Incentive mechanism adjustment: Establish incentive system linked to service performance
  - Supervision system improvement: Ensure power operates in sunlight
- **Result**: Transmission layer returns to core function of "resource regulating valve," enrichment layer becomes system's "risk bearer" and "innovation leader"

### 5. Dynamic Balance of Efficiency and Fairness

- **Core Characteristic**: No egalitarianism, clearly protect reasonable income differences based on labor, management, innovation, risk bearing, while curbing polarization through tax regulation, social security and other mechanisms
- **Implementation Methods**:
  - Primary distribution: Focus on efficiency, encourage innovation and contribution
  - Redistribution: Focus on fairness, regulate income gap through taxation
  - Tertiary distribution: Encourage public welfare and charity, promote social harmony
- **Result**: Achieve dynamic unity of "fairness bottom-line, efficiency improvement"

## 10.3 Implementation Conditions: Institutional Design and Mechanism Guarantee

### 1. Property Rights System

- **Core Requirement**: Clarify public ownership or fair distribution of means of production, eliminate monopolistic possession by individuals or small groups
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Public ownership as main body: Important resources and key fields implement public ownership
  - Multiple ownership coexistence: Encourage healthy development of non-public economy
  - Property rights protection: Legally protect various legitimate property rights

### 2. Supervision Mechanism

- **Core Requirement**: Build diversified rigid supervision system of "all-people supervision + internal self-renewal + third-party evaluation"
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Democratic supervision: Expand citizen participation, enhance supervision breadth
  - Institutional supervision: Establish and improve power restriction and supervision mechanisms
  - Public opinion supervision: Play the supervisory role of media and social public opinion
  - Self-supervision: Establish system's internal self-correction mechanism

### 3. Distribution System

- **Core Requirement**: Implement "distribution according to work as main body, multiple distribution methods coexisting," balancing efficiency and fairness
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Labor remuneration: Ensure labor remuneration matches labor contribution
  - Factor participation in distribution: Allow capital, technology, management and other factors to participate in distribution
  - Social security: Establish social security system covering all people
  - Tax regulation: Regulate excessive income through progressive tax and other methods

### 4. Mobility Mechanism

- **Core Requirement**: Promote education equalization, employment fairness, establish and improve social assistance and upward mobility channels
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Education reform: Promote balanced distribution of educational resources, guarantee education fairness
  - Employment services: Establish fair employment market, eliminate employment discrimination
  - Social assistance: Establish multi-level social assistance system
  - Entrepreneurship support: Provide funding, technology, policy support for entrepreneurs

### 5. Innovation Mechanism

- **Core Requirement**: Increase investment in basic research, technological innovation, improve innovation achievement transformation mechanism
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Technology investment: Increase R&D funding investment, support basic research
  - Talent cultivation: Establish innovation talent cultivation system
  - Achievement transformation: Improve technology achievement transformation mechanism, promote industry-university-research combination
  - Innovation incentives: Establish and improve innovation incentive mechanisms, protect intellectual property

### 6. Cultural Mechanism

- **Core Requirement**: Cultivate cultural values with symbiosis, cooperation, innovation as the core
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Value guidance: Advocate collectivism, cooperative spirit and innovation consciousness
  - Education cultivation: Integrate symbiosis concepts into education system
  - Cultural inheritance: Inherit and innovate excellent traditional culture
  - Public opinion guidance: Create social atmosphere conducive to symbiosis development
