## 2.0 Four-Layer Nested Logic: The Core Theoretical Framework

The Tiandao theory consists of four layers of nested, mutually reinforcing underlying logic, forming a complete theoretical closed loop from the biological foundation to the ultimate cosmic laws:

### Gene Group Evolution Theory

**Core Question**: Where do humans come from? Why are they this way?

**Theoretical Position**: The biological origin of the entire theory, explaining that "the underlying goal of life is survival and replication; individuals are merely survival machines manufactured by genes"

**Core Content**:
- The sole underlying goal of life: survival and replication
- The unit of evolution is not the individual, but the gene group
- Three core mechanisms: kin selection, group selection, reciprocal altruism

### Meme Group Evolution and Domestication Theory

**Core Question**: How does society control people? How are individuals domesticated?

**Theoretical Position**: The bridge connecting the biological foundation to social structure, explaining the spiritual control mechanisms of cultural memes over people; the logical starting point of the entire theory's "anti-domestication, gaining cognitive sovereignty"

**Core Content**:
- Three Laws of Meme Evolution: Transmission priority, meme complex formation, self-protection mechanisms
- Three-layer domestication structure: Emotional anchoring → Identity binding → Meaning monopolization
- Five domestication systems: Morality, romantic love, consumption, success ideology, collectivism

### Social Trophic Level Theory

**Core Question**: How does social structure operate? How does energy flow?

**Theoretical Position**: The most original core of the entire theory, explaining social stratification and energy flow laws

**Core Content**:
- Three-layer trophic structure: Production level, Transmission level, Enrichment level
- Energy flows from bottom to top; the higher the level, the more energy is concentrated
- Morality, law, and culture are essentially "ecological rules" that maintain the stability of energy flow

### Entropy Increase and Tiandao Conservation Law

**Core Question**: What are the ultimate laws of history and destiny?

**Theoretical Position**: The highest level of the theory, explaining the sum of underlying physical + biological + social laws of the universe

**Core Content**:
- Entropy increase: Systems naturally move toward chaos and collapse
- Negative entropy: The only countermeasure is continuous energy input
- Tiandao conservation: Energy can neither be created from nothing nor destroyed into nothing

## 2.1 Meta-Axioms: Underlying Laws of the Universe and Life

Meta-axioms are the logical starting points of the theory, based on cross-disciplinary consensual knowledge, self-evident and uncontroversial:

### Axiom 1: Dissipative Structure Axiom

The universe as a whole tends toward entropy increase (disorder); life and social systems, as local anti-entropy entities (order), are essentially **dissipative structures**. Systems must continuously acquire **negative entropy flow** (matter, energy, information) from the outside and discharge **positive entropy** (waste, heat, chaos) outward to maintain their ordered structure.

- **Physical Basis**: Prigogine's dissipative structure theory
- **Evolutionary Inference**: Once negative entropy input is insufficient or internal entropy discharge is blocked, the system will inevitably disintegrate (death/collapse).

### Axiom 2: Finite Resources in Space-Time Axiom

Within a specific space-time, there is a hard upper limit to the negative entropy flow (resources) that the environment can provide. Although technology can expand resource boundaries, it can never break through instantaneous physical limits.

- **Economic Significance**: Scarcity is eternal; competition is inevitable.
- **Game Theory Inference**: In the stage of resource stock competition, zero-sum games are the default strategy.

### Axiom 3: Group Survival Priority Axiom

The basic unit of evolution is the **gene-meme complex** (group). In long-term evolution, the survival weight of the group is higher than the survival weight of the individual. Individual behaviors that cannot maintain group survival will eventually be eliminated by evolution.

- **Biological Basis**: Group selection theory
- **Ethical Inference**: The essence of "good" is that which benefits group survival; the essence of "evil" is that which harms group survival.

### Axiom 4: Dual Inheritance System Axiom

Humans rely on two systems for inheritance: genes determine physiological instincts (evolving very slowly); memes (institutions, technology, morality, concepts) determine social rules (evolving very quickly), and memes directly determine group fate.

- **Biological Significance**: Gene-meme co-evolution
- **Social Significance**: Cultural memes are the operating system of the social system

## 2.2 Social Evolution Axioms: Laws Specific to Human Society

### Axiom 5: Collaborative Value-Addition Axiom

Through division of labor, collaboration, and innovation, humans can create **incremental resources** far greater than the simple sum of individuals; society is not only about stock competition.

- **Economic Significance**: Division of labor and collaboration improve production efficiency
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Collaboration is the source of group advantage

### Axiom 6: Minimum Energy Path Axiom (Predatory Profit-Seeking)

In systems without strong constraints, energy always flows along the path of least resistance.

- **Predatory Path**: The energy cost of directly appropriating existing resources (predation) is usually lower than creating resources from nothing (production).
- **Inference**: Without institutional constraints, predation is a **thermodynamically inevitable choice**.
- **Social Phenomenon**: As long as benefits > risks, power rent-seeking, capital monopoly, and violent robbery will spontaneously emerge.

### Axiom 7: Meme Legitimization Axiom (Narrative Disguise)

To reduce the systemic friction (resistance costs) generated by predation, beneficiary groups will inevitably evolve a set of **meme narratives** (morality, law, mythology) that package predatory behavior as "justice" or "divine mandate."

- **Function**: This is the **lubricant** of the system, reducing internal energy consumption by lowering cognitive conflict.
- **Evolutionary Significance**: The more successful the predatory structure, the more perfect its legitimizing narrative (it can even deceive the predators themselves).

## 2.3 Two Evolutionary Paths: Predatory Tiandao and Symbiotic Tiandao

Based on the above axioms, human society has two fundamentally different evolutionary paths:

### Predatory Tiandao: Spontaneously Formed Predatory Order

Predatory Tiandao is an **instinctive order without top-level design or constraints**, and has been the mainstream state of human civilization for thousands of years.

#### Core Characteristics

1. **Pyramid Structure**: A stratified structure with many producers at the bottom and few controllers at the top
2. **Class Solidification**: Power, resources, and advantages are transformed into intergenerational transmission
3. **Placebo Channels**: Retaining extremely low-probability upward channels to pacify the lower classes
4. **Instrumentalized Morality**: Morality serves the stability of the predatory system
5. **Technology Increases Efficiency Without Changing Essence**: The increments created by technology are monopolized by the top level first
6. **Endogenous Cyclic Law**: The cycle of predatory imbalance → collapse → reconstruction

#### Evolutionary Fate

- **Cyclical Collapse**: Predation intensity breaks through the survival critical line of the production layer; the system collapses and reconstructs
- **Stagnant Steady State**: Predation intensity is controlled below the survival critical line, forming a low-level steady state

### Symbiotic Tiandao: Anti-Entropy Symbiotic Order

Symbiotic Tiandao is not "naturally occurring" goodness, but a **counter-instinct, high-energy-cost civilizational anti-entropy engineering project**. It is the active resistance against the thermodynamic tendency of "predatory profit-seeking" through precise institutional design, for the long-term survival of the group.

#### Core Characteristics

1. **Anti-Entropy Design**: Must continuously invest energy (regulation, education, transparency) to maintain the "non-predatory" state.
2. **Negative Feedback Regulation**: When wealth polarization (entropy) exceeds a threshold, adjustment mechanisms are automatically triggered (such as progressive taxation, welfare systems).
3. **Symbiotic Structure**: Ensures that the surplus value of the production layer is primarily used for reproduction and innovation, rather than being squandered recklessly by the top level.

#### Evolutionary Advantage (Civilization Filter)

There may be countless civilizations in the universe; Predatory Tiandao is a massive **Great Filter**.
The vast majority of civilizations die from internal entropy increase (collapse caused by excessive predation).
Only civilizations that evolve Symbiotic Tiandao can break through the resource limits of their home planet and enter the interstellar evolution stage.

### Comparison of the Two Paths

| Dimension | Predatory Tiandao | Symbiotic Tiandao |
|-----------|------------------|-------------------|
| Core Logic | Spontaneous predation | Active symbiosis |
| Resource Distribution | One-way concentration | Two-way circulation |
| Class Mobility | Nearly stagnant | Equal opportunity |
| System Stability | Cyclical collapse | Dynamic equilibrium |
| Innovation Vitality | Suppressed | Released |
| Long-term Evolution | Cyclic repetition | Continuous upgrading |

## 2.4 Core Clarifications: Responding to Criticism and Logical Closure

To avoid misunderstanding, this theory makes the following core clarifications:

### Not Pseudoscience

This theory is a social philosophy analytical tool; it does not claim physical-level truth, but only extracts laws from history and reality. "Axiom-Theorem" is a logical deduction tool, not a natural science law.

### Not Absolute Zero-Sum

Acknowledges that technology and collaboration can create increments; what is criticized is "increments being monopolized and preyed upon by the top level," not the denial of increments themselves.

### Does Not Generalize "Predation"

Predation refers only to: **unpaid appropriation of others' labor by monopolizing resources/power**; reasonable returns from venture investment, management, and knowledge-based services are not included.

### Symbiotic Tiandao Is Not Utopia

It only eliminates "internal human predation"; acquiring resources from nature is a biological instinct. The two are completely distinct with no logical contradiction.

### Not a Closed Belief System

Verifiable: The collapse frequency of predatory groups and the survival quality of symbiotic groups can both be tested through history and reality.

### Does Not Deify History

Related practices are **pioneering attempts**, not ultimate singular truths; the complexity and multifacetedness of history are fully respected.
