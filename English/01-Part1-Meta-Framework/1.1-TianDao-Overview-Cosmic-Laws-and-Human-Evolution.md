## 2.0 Four-Layer Nested Logic: Theoretical Core Framework

### First Cornerstone: Gene Group Evolution Theory

**Core Question**: Where do humans come from? Why are they like this?

**Theoretical Position**: The biological origin of the entire theory, explaining "the underlying goal of life is survival and replication, individuals are just survival machines made by genes"

**Core Content**:
- The only underlying goal of life: survival and replication
- The unit of evolution is not the individual, but the gene group
- Three core mechanisms: kin selection, group selection, reciprocal altruism

### Second Cornerstone: Meme Evolution and Domestication Theory

**Core Question**: How does society control people? How are individuals domesticated?

**Theoretical Position**: The bridge connecting the biological bottom layer with social structure, explaining the spiritual control mechanism of cultural memes on humans, is the logical starting point of the entire theory's "anti-domestication, gaining cognitive sovereignty"

**Core Content**:
- Three Laws of Meme Evolution: transmission priority, meme complex formation, self-protection mechanism
- Three-Layer Domestication Structure: emotional anchor → identity → meaning assignment
- Five Major Domestication Systems: morality, love, consumption, success doctrine, collectivism

### Third Cornerstone: Social Trophic Level Theory

**Core Question**: How does social structure operate? How does energy flow?

**Theoretical Position**: The most original core of the entire theory, explaining social stratification and energy flow patterns

**Core Content**:
- Three-layer trophic level structure: production level, transmission level, accumulation level
- Energy flows from bottom to top, the higher the level, the more energy accumulates
- Morality, law, and culture are essentially "ecological rules" that maintain the stability of energy flow

### Fourth Cornerstone: Entropy Increase and TianDao Conservation Law

**Core Question**: What are the ultimate laws of history and destiny?

**Theoretical Position**: The highest level of the theory, explaining the sum of underlying physical + biological + social laws of the universe

**Core Content**:

- Negative entropy: the only way to resist is to continuously input energy
- TianDao conservation: energy is neither created nor destroyed

## 2.1 Meta-Axioms: Cosmic and Life Underlying Laws

Meta-axioms are the logical starting points of the theory, based on interdisciplinary consensus knowledge, self-evident and uncontroversial:

### Axiom 1: Dissipative Structure Axiom

The universe as a whole tends toward entropy increase (disorder), and life and social systems as local reverse-entropy bodies (order) are essentially **dissipative structures**. The system must continuously obtain **negative entropy flow** (matter, energy, information) from the outside and discharge **positive entropy** (waste, heat, chaos) outward to maintain its ordered structure.

- **Physical Basis**: Prigogine's dissipative structure theory
- **Evolutionary Inference**: Once negative entropy input is insufficient or internal entropy discharge is blocked, the system will inevitably disintegrate (death/collapse).

### Axiom 2: Resource Spatiotemporal Limitation Axiom

Within a specific spacetime, there is a hard upper limit to the negative entropy flow (resources) that the environment can provide. Although technology can expand resource boundaries, it can never break through instantaneous physical limits.

- **Economic Significance**: Scarcity is eternal, competition is inevitable.
- **Game Inference**: In the resource stock game stage, zero-sum game is the default strategy.

### Axiom 3: Group Survival Priority Axiom

The basic unit of evolution is the **gene-meme complex** (group). In long-term evolution, the survival weight of the group is higher than the survival weight of the individual. Individual behaviors that cannot maintain group survival will eventually be eliminated by evolution.

- **Biological Basis**: Group selection theory
- **Ethical Inference**: The essence of "good" is beneficial to group survival, the essence of "evil" is harmful to group survival.

### Axiom 4: Dual Inheritance System Axiom

Humans rely on two systems for inheritance: genes determine physiological instincts (evolution is extremely slow); memes (institutions, technology, morality, concepts) determine social rules (evolution is extremely fast), memes directly determine group destiny.

- **Biological Significance**: Gene-meme co-evolution
- **Social Significance**: Cultural memes are the operating system of the social system

## 2.2 Social Evolution Axioms: Human Society-Specific Laws

### Axiom 5: Collaboration Value-Addition Axiom

Through division of labor, collaboration, and innovation, humans can create **incremental resources** far greater than the simple sum of individuals. Society is not just about stock competition.

- **Economic Significance**: Division of labor and collaboration improve production efficiency
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Collaboration is the source of group advantage

### Axiom 6: Energy Minimum Path Axiom (Predation Profit-Seeking)

In a system without strong constraints, energy always flows along the path of least resistance.

- **Predation Path**: The energy consumption of directly possessing existing resources (predation) is usually lower than creating resources from scratch (production).
- **Inference**: Without institutional constraints, predation is a **thermodynamic inevitability**.
- **Social Phenomenon**: As long as benefit > risk, power rent-seeking, capital monopoly, and violent robbery will spontaneously emerge.

### Axiom 7: Meme Legitimization Axiom (Narrative Disguise)

To reduce the system friction (resistance cost) produced by predation, the benefiting group will inevitably evolve a set of **meme narratives** (morality, law, mythology) to package predatory behavior as "justice" or "destiny."

- **Function**: This is the **lubricant** of the system, reducing energy internal friction by reducing cognitive conflict.
- **Evolutionary Significance**: The more successful the predatory structure, the more perfect its legitimizing narrative (it can even deceive the predators themselves).

## 2.3 Two Major Evolutionary Paths: Lower Order Tao and Higher Order Tao

Based on the above axioms, human society has two fundamentally different evolutionary paths:

### Lower Order Tao: Spontaneously Formed Predatory Order

Lower Order Tao is an **instinctual order without top-level design and without constraints**, the mainstream state of human civilization for thousands of years.

#### Core Characteristics

1. **Pyramid Structure**: A stratified structure with many producers at the bottom and few controllers at the top
2. **Class Solidification**: Power, resources, and advantages become intergenerationally transmitted
3. **Placebo Channel**: Retaining a very small probability of upward channel to appease the bottom
4. **Moral Instrumentalization**: Morality serves the stability of the predatory system
5. **Technology Efficiency Enhancement Does Not Change Essence**: The increment created by technology is prioritized and monopolized by the top
6. **Endogenous Cycle Law**: The cycle of predation imbalance → collapse → reconstruction

#### Evolutionary Destiny

- **Cyclic Collapse**: Predation intensity breaks through the survival critical line of the production layer, system collapses and reconstructs
- **Dead Steady State**: Predation intensity is controlled below the survival critical line, forming a low-level steady state

### Higher Order Tao: Reverse-Entropy Work Symbiotic Order

Higher Order Tao is not "naturally occurring" goodness, but a **counter-instinct, high-energy-consumption civilization reverse-entropy engineering**. It is the group's active resistance to the thermodynamic tendency of "predation profit-seeking" through precise institutional design for long-term survival.

#### Core Characteristics

1. **Anti-Entropy Design**: Must continuously input energy (supervision, education, transparency) to maintain the "non-predatory" state.
2. **Negative Feedback Regulation**: When wealth polarization (entropy) exceeds the threshold, automatic adjustment mechanisms are triggered (such as progressive tax, welfare system).
3. **Symbiotic Structure**: Ensure that the surplus value of the production layer is mainly used for reproduction and innovation, rather than being squandered by the top without limit.

#### Evolutionary Advantage (Civilization Filter)

There may be countless civilizations in the universe, and Lower Order Tao is a huge **Great Filter**.
The vast majority of civilizations die from internal entropy increase (collapse caused by excessive predation).
Only civilizations that evolve Higher Order Tao can break through the limits of home planet resources and enter the stage of interstellar evolution.

### Comparison of Two Major Paths

|-----------|-----------------|------------------|
| Core Logic | Spontaneous Predation | Active Symbiosis |
| Resource Distribution | One-way Concentration | Two-way Circulation |
| Class Mobility | Almost Stagnant | Equal Opportunity |

| Innovation Vitality | Suppressed | Released |

## 2.4 Core Clarifications: Responding to Criticism and Logical Closure

To avoid misunderstanding, this theory makes the following core clarifications:

### Not Pseudoscience

This theory is a social philosophy analysis tool, does not claim physical-level truth, only extracts patterns from history and reality. "Axiom-Theorem" is a logical deduction tool, not natural science laws.

### Not Absolute Zero-Sum

Acknowledges that technology and collaboration can create increments; criticizes "increments being monopolized and plundered by the top," not denying the increments themselves.

### Does Not Generalize "Predation"

Predation only refers to: **monopolizing resources/power to gratuitously appropriate others' labor**; reasonable returns such as venture capital, management, and knowledge fees are not included.

### Higher Order Tao is Not Utopia

Only eliminates "human internal predation"; obtaining resources from nature is a life instinct, the two are completely distinguished, there is no logical contradiction.

### Not a Closed Faith

Verifiable: The collapse frequency of predatory groups and the survival quality of symbiotic groups can both be tested through history and reality.

### Does Not Deify History

Related practices are **pioneering attempts**, not the ultimate and only truth, fully respecting the complexity and multifaceted nature of history.
