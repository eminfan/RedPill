# 2 Micro Foundations: Two Core Cornerstones

## Core Proposition

The micro foundation layer is the root of this theory, explaining the underlying logic of "where humans come from and how society operates." It consists of two core cornerstones: **Gene Group Evolution Theory** (First Cornerstone) and **Meme Group Evolution and Domestication Theory** (Second Cornerstone).

The relationship between these two cornerstones: **Gene Group Evolution Theory** answers "where humans come from and why they are this way," while **Meme Group Evolution and Domestication Theory** answers "how society controls people and how individuals are domesticated." The two progress layer by layer, together forming a complete logical chain from biological instinct to social control.

## Positioning of the Two Cornerstones

### First Cornerstone: Gene Group Evolution Theory

**Core Question**: Where do humans come from? Why are they this way?

**Theoretical Position**: Explaining biological instincts and group evolutionary dynamics

**Core Content**:
- The sole underlying goal of life: survival and replication
- The unit of evolution is not the individual, but the gene group
- Three core mechanisms: kin selection, group selection, reciprocal altruism

### Second Cornerstone: Meme Group Evolution and Domestication Theory

**Core Question**: How does society control people? How are individuals domesticated?

**Theoretical Position**: Explaining the spiritual control mechanisms of cultural memes over people; the bridge connecting the biological foundation to social structure, and the logical starting point of the entire theory's "anti-domestication, gaining cognitive sovereignty"

**Core Content**:
- Three Laws of Meme Evolution: Transmission priority, meme complex formation, self-protection mechanisms
- Three-layer domestication structure: Emotional anchoring → Identity binding → Meaning monopolization
- Group-level shell-riding thinking: Distributed cognitive network and group-level cognitive platform
- Five domestication systems: Morality, romantic love, consumption, success ideology, collectivism
- Meme complex immunity to rationality: Self-binding, moral original sin, information cocoon, meaning crushing, backfire effect
- Super-individual integration mechanism: Group crisis activation, three types of super-individual forms
- Group formation mechanism: Selective redundancy, semantic anchoring, false subjectivity
- Anti-domestication practice: Clairvoyance triple penetration, Red Pill awakening, Empty Boat thinking, anti-domestication five-step method
- Positive value anchoring: Examining rather than denying, choosing rather than escaping, creating rather than opposing
- Independent thinking: The complete cognitive process from shell-riding to autonomy

## Theoretical Framework

### I. Dual Encoding Essence Axiom

Humans are life continuation systems jointly constituted and co-evolving by genes and cultural memes:
- **Gene Layer**: Sets underlying biological instincts (five desires), provides the system's energy supply and collaboration prototype
- **Meme Layer**: Constructs super-biological adaptation systems (rules, institutions, culture), expands survival strategy space
- **Synergistic Relationship**: Genes provide the material basis for meme evolution; memes reversely shape gene expression

### II. Group Evolution Priority Axiom

In long-term evolution, the survival weight of the group is higher than the survival weight of the individual. Individual behaviors that cannot maintain group survival will eventually be eliminated by evolution.

### III. Meme Domestication Mechanism Axiom

Social systems achieve coordination of individual behavior through non-violent means; the internalization of social norms makes individual behavior voluntarily coordinated.

### IV. Cognitive Breakthrough Theorem

The cognitive layer is the highest dimension of evolution; individuals can achieve anti-domestication of consciousness and cognitive sovereignty through cognitive awakening.

### V. Energy Conversion Theorem

The stability of social systems depends on the effective conversion of individual energy into system energy; false subjectivity is the key mechanism for achieving this conversion.

### VI. Group Selection Theorem

Characteristics beneficial to group survival will be preserved and strengthened; the evolution of altruistic behavior and the formation of group cohesion are results of group selection.

### VII. Super-Group Competition Theorem

Competition between different civilizations and nations is essentially a survival game of gene-culture complexes, evolving from low-dimensional zero-sum games to high-dimensional non-zero-sum games.

### VIII. Structural Emergence Principle

Social structures are adaptors of gene groups; individuals must adapt to structures to survive, and violating structures results in punishment.

## Theoretical Significance

The micro foundation layer provides a unified theoretical framework for understanding the evolution of human society. It integrates research findings from biology, sociology, cultural studies, and other disciplines into one system, providing new perspectives and methods for explaining various phenomena in human society.

## Practical Application

The theoretical achievements of the micro foundation layer can be applied to multiple fields such as social governance, cultural construction, and educational reform, providing theoretical guidance and practical pathways for solving real-world problems, especially providing systematic methodological support for "anti-domestication and gaining cognitive sovereignty."
