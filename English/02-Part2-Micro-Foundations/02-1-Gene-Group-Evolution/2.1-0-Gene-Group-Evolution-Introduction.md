## Core Axioms

### Axiom One: The Sole Underlying Goal of Life

The sole underlying goal of life is **survival and replication**.

### Axiom Two: The Unit of Evolution Is Not the Individual, but the Gene Group

As long as one can survive and reproduce, it is a successful strategy, regardless of good or evil, beauty or ugliness.

## Three Core Mechanisms

### Mechanism One: Kin Selection

**Definition**: Genes will prioritize protecting relatives who carry the same gene fragments.

**Manifestations**:
- Parents' unconditional devotion to children
- Family solidarity
- Blood loyalty

**Essence**: When you protect your relatives, you are essentially genes protecting their own copies.

### Mechanism Two: Group Selection

**Definition**: When a group faces external threats, "self-sacrifice" behaviors emerge.

**Manifestations**:
- Soldiers charging in battle
- Acting courageously for justice
- Collectivism

**Essence**: Individual death → Group survival → Gene group continuation. Groups without this strategy would be exterminated in competition.

### Mechanism Three: Reciprocal Altruism

**Definition**: You help me, I help you; essentially cross-temporal interest exchange.

**Manifestations**:
- Morality
- Reputation
- Sense of contract

**Essence**: Reducing cooperation costs and improving group survival efficiency.

## Dual-Group Co-Evolution Theory

### Core Proposition

Gene groups and meme groups use individuals as connecting nodes, forming a dual-track co-evolutionary system characterized by "deep symbiosis as the foundation, dynamic gaming as the norm." Understanding this synergistic relationship is key to understanding the underlying operational logic of human society.

### Symbiotic Relationship of Dual Groups

#### Gene Groups Provide Survival Hardware for Meme Groups

- Only humans with developed brains, language ability, and gregarious instincts can become meme hosts
- Without the human biological attributes shaped by gene groups, meme groups have no carriers for existence
- The underlying instincts encoded by gene groups, such as "craving group identity, fearing isolation, pursuing belonging," provide inescapable biological handles for meme group domestication

#### Meme Groups Provide Evolutionary Acceleration for Gene Groups

- Gene group evolution requires hundreds of thousands of years of iteration, while meme group evolution takes only an instant
- It is precisely the collaboration rules, moral systems, and technological inheritance constructed by meme groups that allowed humans to break through the speed limits of gene evolution
- From ordinary mammals to masters of the Earth, greatly enhancing the survival probability and expansion capability of gene groups

#### Deep Nested Binding

Top-level communities that can survive across millennia are all deep symbionts of gene groups and meme groups:
- A nation is both a gene group sharing a core gene pool and a meme group sharing culture, values, and historical narratives
- The two are deeply bound and mutually supportive

### Gaming Relationship of Dual Groups

#### Goal Divergence

The core goals of the two have fundamental divergence:
- The ultimate goal of gene groups: Cross-generational replication and survival of the gene pool
- The ultimate goal of meme groups: Scale expansion and survival of the meme pool
- The two are not always consistent

#### Typical Gaming Cases

**"DINK, non-marriage, non-reproduction" meme groups**:
- Cause individuals to voluntarily abandon reproduction
- Directly violate the core survival goal of gene groups

**"Celibate cultivation, sacrificing life for ideals" meme groups**:
- Cause individuals to actively avoid gene instincts and even abandon life
- Completely break through the underlying encoding of gene groups

#### Individual-Level Manifestation

The daily struggles, internal friction, and mental conflicts of individuals are essentially the external manifestations of the game between gene groups and meme groups on the individual as host.

### Nested Binding Mechanism of Dual Groups

#### Mutual Adaptation

Meme groups that can stably survive will inevitably precisely interface with the underlying instincts of gene groups:
- "Family-nation sentiment" memes interface with group survival instincts
- "Consumerism" memes interface with resource acquisition and sexual competition instincts

#### Co-Iteration

Gene groups also complete iterative screening in the environment constructed by meme groups:
- "Socialized collaboration" meme groups give individuals who are better at empathy and collaboration more reproductive opportunities
- In turn, this screens the corresponding gene pool, achieving co-iteration of the two

### Individual's Dual Host Attribute

Individuals are innately endowed with dual host attributes, serving as dual connection carriers for gene groups and meme groups:

| Level | Attribute | Essence |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Biological Level | Replication copy of the gene group | Biological host for the gene group to achieve cross-generational survival |

**Core Insight**: The underlying instincts, the vast majority of thoughts, daily behaviors, and life choices of individuals are essentially concrete manifestations of the dual groups' survival goals, not individual independent will.

### Society as the Ultimate Carrier of Dual Groups

The society we live in is essentially the ultimate evolutionary carrier of gene groups and meme groups nested and bound, with individuals as nodes:
- **The bottom layer of society**: One or more symbiotic gene groups
- **The upper layer of society**: A stable meme group covering all individuals
- **The nodes of society**: Countless individuals as gene copies and meme terminals, connecting the two
- **The rules of society**: The group-level consensus framework retained after countless rounds of competitive screening by dual groups

Dynasty changes and institutional transformations never alter this underlying structure; they are merely operating system updates of meme groups and compositional adjustments of gene groups.
