## 12.1 Symbiotic Tiandao Theorem: The Possibility of Proactive Construction

### Core Proposition

**Symbiotic Tiandao is a proactive construction based on recognizing laws, with the group's long-term survival as the goal, without violating any foundational axioms.**

Symbiotic Tiandao is not a once-and-for-all static ultimate form, but an evolutionary practice process in which humanity, based on continuously deepening cognition of Tiandao's objective laws, continuously trials and errors, iterates, and dynamically optimizes. Its complete definition is:

**A symbiotic social order that humanity, after thoroughly recognizing the cyclical fate of lower-order predatory order, proactively designs and continuously optimizes based on the core goal of "maximizing the group's long-term survival and evolutionary advantage." It is not a perfect system built at once, but a dynamic evolutionary process that is always practicing, always cognizing, and always correcting; its core vitality lies not in error-free top-level design but in possessing an endogenous self-correction mechanism that always returns to the core goal.**

### Symbiotic Tiandao Theorem System

#### 1. Compliance Theorem

**Symbiotic Tiandao does not negate survival needs, resource limitations, group evolution, or other foundational laws; it only reconstructs resource distribution and power rules.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Meta-axiom system
- **Core Logic**: Achieving better resource distribution through institutional design while respecting objective laws
- **Practical Significance**: Avoiding utopian fantasy, ensuring theoretical feasibility

#### 2. Anti-Predation Theorem

**Abolish through institutions "uncompensated appropriation based on monopoly of means of production/power"; there is no institutionalized predation within humanity, only equivalent exchange and reasonable division-of-labor returns.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Predatory Profit-Seeking Axiom
- **Core Logic**: Curbing predatory behavior through institutional design, ensuring fair resource distribution
- **Practical Significance**: Eliminating the system's endogenous root cause, achieving sustainable development

#### 3. Group Advantage Theorem

**Incremental resources are directed toward universal education, healthcare, innovation, infrastructure, and security; overall vitality, risk resistance, and long-term survival quality far exceed lower-order systems.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Collaborative Value-Addition Axiom
- **Core Logic**: Enhancing the group's overall quality and capability through public investment
- **Practical Significance**: Releasing the group's innovation potential, improving the system's overall competitiveness

#### 4. Anti-Solidification Theorem

**Class mobility is based solely on individual capability, labor, and contribution, blocking intergenerational monopoly advantages and maintaining stable mobility.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Reverse design of the Class Solidification Theorem
- **Core Logic**: Establishing fair competition mechanisms, ensuring equal opportunity
- **Practical Significance**: Maintaining social vitality, avoiding system rigidity caused by class solidification

#### 5. Cycle-Breaking Theorem

**Eliminating the endogenous collapse driver of "predation infinitely escalating," resource distribution becomes sustainable, no longer falling into the cycle of predatory Tiandao.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Reverse design of the Endogenous Cycle Theorem
- **Core Logic**: Achieving balanced energy flow through institutional design
- **Practical Significance**: Breaking through the historical cycle law, achieving sustained civilizational development

#### 6. Dynamic Iteration Theorem

**Humanity's cognition of Tiandao's objective laws always has stage-specific limitations; the external environment and internal structure of group evolution are always in dynamic change; therefore, the practice of symbiotic Tiandao inevitably follows the spiral ascent pattern of "practice-cognition-deviation-correction-deepened cognition-iterative upgrading." The long-term evolutionary advantage of symbiotic Tiandao does not depend on whether the one-time constructed system is perfect, but on whether it possesses endogenous self-correction and self-revolution mechanisms that can consistently adhere to the core goal of "maximizing the group's long-term survival and evolutionary advantage" and continuously correct deviations in practice.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Extension and expansion of the meta-axiom system
- **Core Logic**: Trial and error and correction are inevitable components of symbiotic Tiandao practice; self-correction and self-iteration are its core vitality
- **Practical Significance**: Providing a theoretical foundation for accommodating mistakes in historical practice, ensuring the theory's dynamic openness

### Key Distinction: Essential Goal vs. Practice Deviation

In understanding the historical practice of symbiotic Tiandao, two core concepts that cannot be conflated must be clarified:

- **The Essential Goal of Symbiotic Tiandao**: With gene group long-term survival as the core, achieving zero institutionalized predation, universal resource access, and maximized group collaboration efficiency, thoroughly breaking out of the historical cycle law of predatory Tiandao. This goal conforms to Tiandao's meta-axiom system and is objective, stable, and unshakeable.

- **Behavioral Deviations in the Practice Process of Symbiotic Tiandao**: In exploring paths to achieve the core goal, behaviors that violate Tiandao's core axioms and deviate from the core goal due to cognitive limitations, environmental constraints, and insufficient experience. These are stage-specific and correctable, and do not represent the theoretical essence of symbiotic Tiandao as invalid.

1. **Returning to the Ontology of Anti-Entropy Survival: Centering on Economic Construction, Solidifying the Material Foundation of Group Evolution**

Addressing the deviation of "violating the resource space-time limitation axiom, subjectivism breaking through objective boundaries," the core correction of reform and opening up was to completely abandon the erroneous line of "taking class struggle as the key link" and establish the basic line of "taking economic construction as the center," returning to Tiandao's ultimate ontology of "survival is anti-entropy." Through the rural household responsibility system, urban state-owned enterprise reform, and socialist market economy system construction, it fully respects the objective laws of economic development and improves anti-entropy efficiency through division of labor, collaboration, and institutional innovation within resource boundaries.

2. **Reconstructing the Meme Adaptation System: The Core Thesis of the Primary Stage of Socialism, Achieving Precise Matching of Memes and Evolutionary Stage**
